Verizon Communications, Inc. is a holding company, which engages in the provision of broadband and communication services. It operates through the following segments: Wireless and Wireline. The Wireless segment provides wireless communications services and products on a postpaid and prepaid basis which are provided to consumer, business, and government customers. The Wireless segment offers broadband video and data; corporate networking solutions; data center and cloud services; security and managed network services; andRead More local & long distance voice services. The company was founded on June 30, 2000 and is headquartered in New York, NY.
