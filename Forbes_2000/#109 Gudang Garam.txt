PT Gudang Garam Tbk engages in the manufacture and sale of cigarettes. The company operates through the following segments: Cigarettes, Paperboard, and Others. It produces clove cigarettes including low-tar and low-nicotine variants, and the traditional hand-rolled kretek. The company was founded by Surya Wonowidjojo on June 26, 1958 and is headquartered in Kediri, Indonesia.
