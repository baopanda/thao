United States Steel Corp. engages in the manufacture and sale of steel products. It operates through the following segments: Flat-Rolled Products, U.S. Steel Europe and Tubular Products. The Flat-Rolled Products segment manages steel plants and production facilities that manufactures steel slabs, rounds, strip mill plates, sheets, tin mill, iron ore, and coke. The U.S. Steel Europe segment produces and markets strip mill plates, spiral welded pipe, heating radiators, refractory ceramic materials. The Tubular ProductsRead More segment manufactures and trades seamless and electric resistance welded steel casing and tubing. line pipe, and mechanical tubing. The company was founded in 1901 by Andrew Carnegie, John Pierpont Morgan, Charles Michael Schwab, and Elbert Henry Gary and is headquartered in Pittsburgh, PA.
