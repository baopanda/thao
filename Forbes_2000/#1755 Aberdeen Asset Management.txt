Aberdeen
Aberdeen Asset Management Plc is a global investment management company, managing assets for both institutional and retail clients from offices around the world. The company invests in spans equities, fixed income, property and alternative investment strategies. The company's clients include national and corporate pension funds, central banks and other investment institutions. Aberdeen Asset Management was founded by Martin James Gilbert on March 2, 1983 and is headquartered in Aberdeen, the United Kingdom.
