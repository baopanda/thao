China Southern Airlines Co. Ltd. engages in domestic and international airline services. It operates through a single segment: Airline Business, which provides air cargo and mail services. The company's services include airport, in-flight, flight information, online booking, boarding, kiosk, baggage and cargo. China Southern Airlines was founded on March 25, 1995 and is headquartered in Guangzhou, China.
