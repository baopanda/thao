Geberit AG develops, manufactures, and distributes sanitary products and systems for the residential and industrial construction industry. It offers sanitary systems such as installation systems, cisterns and mechanisms, faucets and flushing systems, and waste fittings and traps. It also sells piping systems such as building drainage and supply systems. The company was founded by Caspar Melchior Albert Gebert in 1874 and is headquartered in Rapperswil-Jona, Switzerland.
