Thai Beverage Public Co., Ltd. is a holding company, which engages in the production and distribution of alcoholic and non-alcoholic beverages. It operates through the following segments: Spirits, Beer, Non-Alcoholic Beverages, and Food. The Spirits segment produces and sells spirit products including brown and white spirits. The Beer segment produces and sells beer products. The Non-Alcoholic Beverage segment produces and sells branded water, soda, ready-to-drink coffee, energy drink, green tea and fruit flavoured drinks.Read More The Food segment distributes foods and beverages, and manages Japanese restaurants. The company was founded on October 29, 2003 and is headquartered in Bangkok, Thailand.
