BNP Paribas SA engages in the provision of banking and financial services. It operates through the following main businesses: Retail Banking and Services and Corporate and Institutional Banking. The Retail Banking and Services business includes domestic markets and international financial services. The Corporate and Institutional Banking comprises of corporate banking, global markets, and securities services. The company was founded on May 23, 2000 and is headquartered in Paris, France.
