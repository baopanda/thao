Hanjin Shipping Co. Ltd. is engaged in the provision of marine transportation services. It services include container, bulk, and terminal. The container service provides U.S. inland service, reefer, dangerous, over-sized cargos, equipment, and container vessel fleet. The bulk service includes trampers, tanker, dedicated services, and bulk vessel fleet.  The termincal service offers logistics services. The company was founded in September 1949, and is headquartered in Seoul, South Korea.
