The Bank of Chongqing Co. Ltd. provides commercial banking services. It operates through four segments: Corporate Banking, Retail Banking, Treasury and Unallocated. The Corporate Banking segment provides corporate customers with financial products and services including deposits and loans. The Retail Banking segment provides individual customers with financial products and services including deposits and loans. The Treasury segment offers inter-bank lending and borrowing, bonds investment, re-purchasing and foreign currencyRead More transactions. The Unallocated segment performs the businesses not included in the above segments. The company was founded on September 27, 1996 and is headquartered in Chongqing, China.
