Estée Lauder Cos., Inc. manufactures and markets skin care, makeup, fragrance, and hair care products. It offers skin care products, such as moisturizers, serums, cleansers, toners, body care, exfoliators, acne and oil correctors, facial masks, cleansing devices, and sun care products; fragrance products, including lotions, powders, creams, candles, and soaps; makeup products, and other ancillary products.
