CP All Public Co. Ltd. engages in the operation of convenience stores in Thailand under the 7-Eleven trademark. The firm is also involved in the bill payment service business; distribution and repair of retail equipment; and manufacture and sale of frozen food and bakery. It also provides information technology, marketing consulting, logistics, educational, and training and seminar services. The company was founded in 1988 and is headquartered in Bangkok, Thailand.
