Finatis SA operates as a holding company with interests in the commercial property investment, food and sport goods distribution. The company operates a network of multi-format food stores in France and internationally. It distributes sporting goods under the brand names: Go Sport and Courir in France and Poland. The company is headquartered in Paris, France.
