Axiata Group Bhd. is an investment holding company, which engages in the provision of mobile communication and network transmission related services. It also offers technical and management services on an international scale with investments in subsidiaries, joint ventures, and associates. The company was founded on June 12, 1992 and is headquartered in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia.
