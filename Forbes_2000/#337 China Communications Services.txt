China Communications Services Corp. Ltd. engages in the provision of integrated support services in the information sector, including telecommunications, media and technology. The company offers telecommunications infrastructure services, including design, construction and project supervision and management; business process outsourcing services, including network maintenance, facilities management and distribution of telecommunications services and products; applications, content and other services, including ITRead More applications, mobile Internet services and value-added voice services.
