Ezdan Real Estate Co. engages in the acquisition and sale of property and land, general contracting, property consulting services, and investments services. It operates through the following  segments: Residential and Commercial Property, Investments, Hotel & Suites, and Malls. The Residential and Commercial Property segment develops, owns, trades, and rent real estate properties. The Investments segment engages in the investing activities. The Hotels and Suites segment manages hotels, suites, and restaurants. The MallsRead More segment includes management of malls. The company was founded by Thani bin Abdullah Al-Thani in 1960 and is headquartered in Doha, Qatar.
