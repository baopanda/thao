NiSource, Inc. operates as an energy holding company, which through its subsidiaries, engages in the provision of natural gas, electricity, and other products and services. It operates through the Gas Distribution Operations and Electric Operations segments. The Gas Distribution Operations segment focuses on the provision of natural gas service and transportation for residential, commercial, and industrial customers in Ohio, Pennsylvania, Virginia, Kentucky, Maryland, and Massachusetts.
