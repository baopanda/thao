Shionogi & Co., Ltd. engages in the research, development, supply, manufacture, and sale of pharmaceutical products. Its products include prescription drugs, over-the-counter drugs, and diagnostics. The company was founded by Gisaburo Shiono Sr. on March 17, 1878 and is headquartered in Osaka, Japan.
