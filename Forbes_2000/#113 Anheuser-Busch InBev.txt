Anheuser-Busch InBev SA engages in the provision of beverages products. Its activities include manufacturing, marketing and distribution of alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks. It operates through the following geographical segments: North America, Mexico, and Latin America North, Latin America South, Europe, Asia Pacific and Global Export, and Holding Company. The company was founded on August 2, 1977 and is headquartered in Leuven, Belgium.
