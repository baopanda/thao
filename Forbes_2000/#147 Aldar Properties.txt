Aldar Properties PJSC engages in the development, sales, investment, construction, management and associated services for real estate. The company operates through six business segments: Property Development and Sales, Investment Properties Portfolio, Operative Village, Hotels, Schools and Leisure. It also engages in the development and operation of hotels, schools, marinas, golf courses and theme parks. The company was founded on October 12, 2004 and is headquartered in Abu Dhabi, the United Arab Emirates.
