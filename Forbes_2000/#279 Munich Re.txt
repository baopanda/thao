Münchener Rückversicherungs AG engages in the provision of insurance and reinsurance services. It operates through the following segments: Life Reinsurance, Property-Casualty Reinsurance, Life Primary Insurance, Health Primary Insurance, Property-Casualty Primary Insurance, Munich Health, and Asset Management. The company was founded by Carl von Thieme on April 3, 1880 and is headquartered in Munich, Germany.
