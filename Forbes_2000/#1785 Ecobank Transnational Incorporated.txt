Ecobank Transnational, Inc. provides wholesale, retail, investment and transactional banking services. It operates through two segments: Domestic Bank & Corporate and Investment Bank.  The Domestic Bank segment provides convenient, accessible and reliable financial products and services to individuals, small businesses, local corporates and public sector organizations. The Corporate & Investment Bank segment provides financial solutions to global, regional and public corporates, financial institutions and internationalRead More organisations. Its products and services include pan-African lending, trade services, cash management, internet banking and value-chain finance. This segment also provides treasury services, corporate finance, investment banking and securities and asset management. Ecobank Transnational was founded in 1985 and is headquartered in Lom, Togo.
