Telefónica SA is an integrated telecommunications operator, providing communication, information and entertainment solutions. It provides Multinational, Large Enterprise, SME and Wholesale solutions. The company provides its products under the brands of Movistar, O2, Vivo. The company is also provides Global Resources and Digital services. Telefónica was founded on April 19, 1924 and is headquartered in Madrid, Spain.
