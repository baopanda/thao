Saudi Telecom Co. engages in the provision of telecommunication services. It operates through the following segments: GSM, PSTN, DATA, and Un-allocated. The GSM segment offers mobile, third and fourth generation services, prepaid cards, international roaming, and messages services. The PSTN segment concerns fixed lines, telephone cards, interconnects, and international calls services. The DATA segment includes leased data transmission circuits, DSL and internet services. The Un-allocated segment contains items which couldRead More not be linked with the main operating segments of the Group. The company was founded on April 21, 1998 and is headquartered in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia.
