Booking Holdings, Inc. is an online travel company, which provides travel and related services. It offers accommodation reservations including hotels, bed and breakfasts, hostels, apartments, vacation rentals and other properties. The company provide services through Booking.com, priceline.com, agoda.com, KAYAK, rentalcars.com and OpenTable brands. It also allows consumers to easily compare airline ticket, hotel reservation and rental car reservation information from hundreds of travel websites at once through KAYAK.
