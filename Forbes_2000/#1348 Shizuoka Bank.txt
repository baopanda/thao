The Shizuoka Bank, Ltd. engages in the provision of financial banking services. It operates through the following segments: Banking Operations, Leasing Operations, and Others. The Banking Operations segment consists of deposits, loans, investment securities, and exchange transactions. The Leasing Operations segment includes lease transactions centered on finance leases. The Others segment includes commissioned computer processing, domestic financial commodity trading, and credit guarantee services.
