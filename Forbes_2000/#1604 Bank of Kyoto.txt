The Bank of Kyoto, Ltd. engages in the provision of financial services. It operates through the Banking Business and Others segments. The Banking Business segment includes deposits and loans services, trading securities, securities investment, and domestic and foreign exchange services. The Others segment includes credit guarantee, lease, and credit card businesses. The company was founded on October 1, 1941 and is headquartered in Kyoto, Japan
