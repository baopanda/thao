Sumitomo Realty & Development Co., Ltd. engages in the real estate business. It operates through the following segments: Leasing, Sales, Construction, Brokerage, and Other. The Leasing segment consists and manages leasing of office buildings, residences and other properties. The Sales segment segment consists of condominiums, detached houses, and land lots. The Construction segment handles construction of houses, condominiums, and buildings. The Brokerage segment deals with real estate brokerage services.
