The Bank of Iwate, Ltd. engages in the regional banking business. It provides deposits, loans, local and foreign exchange, trading securities sales, corporate bonds, and registration services. It also engages in other financial services such as computerized processing consignment, lease, credit card, and credit guarantee. The company was founded on May 2, 1932 and is headquartered in Morioka, Japan.
