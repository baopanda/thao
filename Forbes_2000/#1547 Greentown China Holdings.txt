Greentown China Holdings Ltd. is an investment holding company, which engages in the property development activities. It operates through the four segments: Property Development, Hotel Operations, Property Investment and Others. The Property Development segment involves in the development and sale of residential properties. The Hotel Operations segment includes management and control of hotels. The Property Investment segment involves in the investment of properties for income. The Others segment includes sales ofRead More construction materials, electronic engineering, design and decoration, and project management. The company was founded by Wei Ping Song in January 1995 and is headquartered in Hangzhou, China.
