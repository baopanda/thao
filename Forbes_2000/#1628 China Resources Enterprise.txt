China Resources Beer (Holdings) Co. Ltd. operates as an investment holding company, which engages in the retailing of beer, food and beverage businesses. It operates through the following business segments: Retail, Beer, Food and Beverage. The Retail segment operates multi format stores, including hypermarkets, supermarkets, convenience stores and specialty stores. Its brands include Fun2, Ole, blt, Vango, CR Vanguard, Suguo, Voi_la, Chinese Arts & Crafts, CR Care, Vivo and Pacific Coffee. The Beer segment sells beer underRead More the Snow brand. The Food segment operated via its arm, CR Ng Fung, which is a food conglomerate engaging in food research and development, production, processing, wholesaling, retailing, logistics and international trading. The Beverage segment operates beverage plants in China. It markets purified water under the C'estbon brand. The company also invests in properties. The company was founded in 1992 and is headquartered in Hong Kong.
