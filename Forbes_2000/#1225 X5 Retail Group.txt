X5 Retail Group NV operates as a holding company, which engages in the provision of retail grocery stores. It also operates a retail chain of economy class, supermarket, hypermarket and expresses stores under the brand names Pyaterochka, Perekresto, Karuse and Perekrestok Express. The company was founded on August 13, 1975 and is headquartered in Amsterdam, Netherlands.
