Taiwan Mobile Co. Ltd. engages in the provision of ""Quadruple Play"" services covering mobile, fixed-line, cable television and broadband. Its products and services include postpaid service, prepaid service, international call, and international roaming. The company was founded on February 25, 1997 and is headquartered in Taipei, Taiwan.
