Hopson Development Holdings Ltd. operates as an investment holding company, which engages in the real estate properties. The company focuses on property developments such as residential property, commercial property and hotel property, and the tourist and vacation business and property management business. It operates its business through four segments: Property Development, Property Investment, Hotel Operations and Property Management. The company was founded in 1992 and is headquartered in Hong Kong.
