KazMunaiGas Exploration Production JSC engages in the acquisition, exploration, development, production, processing and export of hydrocarbons with its core operations of oil and gas properties. The company conducts its principal operations through the UzenMunaiGas and EmbaMunaiGas production divisions. It produces crude oil, natural and associated gas from its hydrocarbon reserves on-shore in Kazakhstan. KazMunaiGas Exploration Production was founded in March 2004 and is headquartered in Astana, Kazakhstan.
