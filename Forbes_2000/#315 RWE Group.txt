RWE AG is a holding company, which engages in the generation, transmission, distribution, and trading of electricity and gas. It operates through the following segments: Conventional Power Generation; Supply/Distribution Networks Germany; Supply Netherlands/Belgium; Central Eastern and South Eastern Europe; Renewables; Supply United Kingdom; Trading/Gas Midstream; and Other Consolidation. The company was founded in 1898 and is headquartered in Essen, Germany.
