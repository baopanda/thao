Datang International Power Generation Co., Ltd. engages in electricity power business. It provides services such as construction and operation of power plants, sale of electricity and steam, proliferation and maintenance as well as repair of power generation equipment and other power technology services. The company operates through the following segments: Power Generation, Coal, Chemical, and Other. Datang International Power Generation was founded on December 13, 1994 and is headquartered in Beijing, China.
