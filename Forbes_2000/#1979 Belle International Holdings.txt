Belle International Holdings Ltd. engages in the manufacture and trade of clothing apparel and footwear products. It operates through the two segments: Shoes and Footwear Products; and Sportswear and Apparel Products. The Shoes and Footwear Products segment produces and distributes footwear goods under the following brands: Belle, Stacatto, Joy & Peace, MIllie's, Jipi Japa, Mirabell, Tata, Teenmix, Senda, Basto, Skap, 15mins, and Map by Belle. The Sportswear and Apparel Products segment sells under the following licensedRead More brands: Nike, Adidas, Puma, Converse, Mizuno, Moussy, and Sly. The company was founded in 1978 and is headquartered in Hong Kong.
