PT Perusahaan Gas Negara (Persero) Tbk is engaged in the distribution and transmission of natural gas to industrial, commercial, and household customers. It operates through the following business segments: Transmission and Transportation; Distribution and Trading; and Others. The Transmission and Transportation business segment transports natural gas through high pressure transmission pipeline to transfer point. The Distribution and Trading business segment distributes gas purchased from suppliers and supplies toRead More industrial, commercial, and household users. The Others business segment provides fiber optic capacity leasing, and construction and maintenance services. The company was founded in May 13, 1965 and is headquartered in Jakarta, Indonesia.
