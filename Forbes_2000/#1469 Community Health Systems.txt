Community Health Systems, Inc. engages in the management and operation of general acute care hospitals. It operates through two segments: The Hospital Operations and Home Care Agency Operations. The Hospital Operations segment provides inpatient and outpatient healthcare services. The Home Care Agency Operations segment includes in-home outpatient care services. The company was founded in March 1985 and is headquartered in Franklin, TN.
