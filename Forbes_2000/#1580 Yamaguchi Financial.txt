Yamaguchi Financial Group, Inc. provides banking and other financial services. It operates through the Banking and Other Business divisions. The Banking division offers deposits, loans, domestic and foreign exchange, securities trading, and investment securities. The Other division includes securities, credit card, and leasing business. The company was founded on October 2, 2006 and is headquartered in Shimonoseki, Japan.
