Chang Hwa Commercial Bank, Ltd. engages in commercial banking transactions. It provides trust services, international banking, and overseas branch banking. Other activities include deposits, bank debentures, loans, discounted bills and notes, corporate and government bonds, domestic and foreign remittances, commercial drafts, and letters of credit. The company was founded by Ju Hsiang Wu on June 5, 1905 and is headquartered in Taichung, Taiwan.
