Church & Dwight Co., Inc. engages in the development, manufacture, and market of household, personal care, and specialty products. It operates through the following segments: Consumer Domestic, Consumer International, and Specialty Products. The Consumer Domestic segment includes the power brands such as KABOOM cleaning products, ARRID antiperspirant, CLOSE-UP and AIM toothpastes, and SIMPLY SALINE nasal saline moisturizer. The Consumer International segment offers personal care products. The Specialty Products segmentRead More involves in producing sodium bicarbonate. The company was founded by Dwight John and Austin Church in 1846 and is headquartered in Ewing, NJ.
