East Japan Railway Co. engages in the business of railway transportation services. It operates through the following segments: Transportation, Station Space Utilization, Shopping Centers and Office Buildings, and Others. The Transportation segment engages in the passenger transportation mainly the railways operations. The Station Space Utilization segment handles the creation of commercial spaces at stations for various businesses such as retail stores, restaurants, and convenience stores. The Others segment involves in hotel operations and advertising agency. The company was founded on April 1, 1987 and is headquartered in Tokyo, Japan.
