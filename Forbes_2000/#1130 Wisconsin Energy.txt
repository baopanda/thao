Wisconsin Energy Corp. provides electric generation and natural gas distribution services. The company operates its business through two operating segments: Utility Energy and Non-Utility Energy. The Utility Energy segment consists of Wisconsin Electric and Wisconsin Gas. Wisconsin Electric serves electric customers in Wisconsin and the Upper Peninsula of Michigan, gas customers in Wisconsin and steam customers in metropolitan Milwaukee, Wisconsin.
