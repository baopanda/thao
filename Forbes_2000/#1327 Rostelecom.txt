Rostelecom Long-Distance & Intl Telecommunications Co. OJSC engages in the provision of communication services, data transmission, internet, pay television, video private network, data center services, and radio communication services. The communication services include local, intra-zone, long-distance domestic and international fixed-line telephone services, and mobile services. The company was founded on September 23, 1993 and is headquartered in Moscow, Russia.
