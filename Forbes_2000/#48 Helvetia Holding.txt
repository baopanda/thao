Helvetia Holding AG provides insurance and financial services. The company offers life insurance products that cover retirement and pension plans. It also offers non-life insurance which focuses on property; vehicle; liability and transportation; and health and accident insurance. The company was founded in 1858 and is headquartered in St. Gallen, Switzerland.
