The shikoku Bank, Ltd. engages in the provision of financial services. Its operatons are carried out through the Banking and Other segments. The Banking segment deals with commercial banking services, such as deposits, loans, foreign exchange, insurance business, mutual funds, and leasing. The Other segment consists of real estate maintenance business, computer-related business, and industrial, economic and financial research studies and venture capital.
