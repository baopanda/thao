Gunma Bank, Ltd. engages in the provision of financial services. It operates through the following segments: Banking, Lease, and Others. The Banking segment offers deposits, loans, securities, investments, domestic and foreign exchange, and trust services. The Leasing segment engages in financial lease transactions. The Others segment includes transport of goods, maintenance of automated teller machines, and insurance business. The company was founded on September 14, 1932 and is headquartered in Maebashi, Japan.
