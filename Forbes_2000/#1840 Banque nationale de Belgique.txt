Banque Nationale de Belgique SA engages in the implementation of monetary policies and circulation of banknotes. It defines, monitors, and implements the Eurosystem's monetary policies; prints, issues, and circulates banknotes and coins; collects, compiles, analyzes, and distributes economic and financial information; and practices financial supervision to ensure financial and monetary stability. The company was founded on May 5, 1850 and is headquartered in Brussels, Belgium.
