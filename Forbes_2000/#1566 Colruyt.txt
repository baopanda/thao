Colruyt SA engages in the retail and distribution of food and non-food products. It operates through the following segments: Retail, Wholesale and Food Service, and Other Activities. The Retail segment includes stores under the management that supplies retail customers directly. The Wholesale and Food Service segment supplies wholesalers, bulk customers, and affiliated independent merchants. The Other Activities segment includes operations of gas stations, printing and engineering activities, and alternative energy.
