Hyundai Wia Corp. engages in the production and sale of automotive parts. It operates through the following business areas: Machinery, Automotive Parts, Defense, Industrial Machinery, Satellite, and Global Business. The Machinery business provides machine tools, factory automation equipment, and industrial robots. The Automotive Parts business manufactures automotive module, engine, and parts. The Defense business offers firearms and aircraft parts. The Industrial Machinery business produces plants and press. The SatelliteRead More business provides space image information production services. The Global business offers machinery, automotive parts, automotive engine, components and materials, and molding businesses. The company was founded on March 29, 1976 and is headquartered in Changwon, South Korea.
