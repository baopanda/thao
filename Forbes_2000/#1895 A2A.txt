A2A SpA engages in the production, sale, and distribution of gas, electricity, district heating, environmental services, and the integrated water cycle. It operates through the following segments: Energy, Heat and Services, Environment, Networks, and Corporate and Other Services. The Energy segment engages in the sale of electricity and natural gas on the wholesale and retail electricity markets, as well as the methane gas market. The Heat and Services segment engages in the sale of heat and electricity produced byRead More cogeneration plants. The Environment segment involves the whole waste management cycle, which includes the collection, treatment, disposal, and recovery of materials and energy. The Networks segment consists of the operational management of networks in the distribution of electricity, the transport and distribution of natural gas, and the management of the entire integrated water cycle. The Corporate and Other Services segment includes various activities such as the guidance, strategic direction, coordination, and control of industrial operations. The company was founded on January 1, 2008 is headquartered in Brescia, Italy.
