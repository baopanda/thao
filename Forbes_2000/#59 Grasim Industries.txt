Grasim Industries Ltd. manufactures viscose staple fibre, cement, chemicals and textiles. It operates through four segments: Fibre and Pulp, Chemicals and Others. The Fibre and Pulp segment offers viscose staple fibre and rayon grade pulp, which is a man-made, biodegradable fibre with characteristics akin to cotton. The Chemicals segment produces caustic soda and other allied chemicals. The Others segment mainly manufactures textiles such as fabrics and synthetic yarns.
