Formosa Petrochemical Corp. engages in the production, exploration and refinery of crude oil and other petroleum products. Its products include gasoline, diesel, LPG, Kerosene, Naphtha, Petrochemical Raw Material, Fuel Oil/ Asphalt/ Sulfur, Base Oil, and Lubricant. The company was founded on April 6, 1992 and is headquartered in Taipei, Taiwan.
