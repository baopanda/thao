KEYENCE Corp. engages in the development, manufacture and sale of industrial automation and inspection equipment. Its products include various sensors and measuring equipment, programmable controllers, touch panels, bar code readers, microscopes, recorders, laser markers, three dimensional printers, static eliminators, and packaged software. The company was founded by Takemitsu Takizaki in March 1972 and is headquartered in Osaka, Japan
