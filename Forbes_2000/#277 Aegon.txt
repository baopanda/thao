AEGON NV operates as a holding company, which engages in the provision of insurance, pensions, and asset management services. It operates through the following segments: Americas, The Netherlands, United Kingdom (""UK""), New Markets, and Holding and Other Activities. Its products include life and non-life insurance on accident, health, car, and fire. The company was founded in 1983 and is headquartered in The Hague, Netherlands.
