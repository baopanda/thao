The 77 Bank, Ltd. engages in the provision of banking and financial services. It operates through the following segments: Banking, Leasing, and Other. The Banking segment provides customers with banking services including deposit, lending, and exchange. The Leasing segment offers customers with leasing services. The Other segment consists of credit guarantee and credit card businesses. The company was founded on December 9, 1878 and is headquartered in Sendai, Japan.
