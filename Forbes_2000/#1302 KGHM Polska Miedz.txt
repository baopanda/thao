KGHM Polska Miedz SA is engaged in the mining and processing of copper and silver. The company operates its business through six segments: KGHM Polska Miedz, KGHM INTERNATIONAL, Sierra Gorda Project, Resource Base Development, Support of the Core Business and Other Segments. The KGHM Polska Miedz segment engages produces copper and silver. The KGHM INTERNATIONAL segment comprises the operation of functioning mines extracting copper. The Sierra Gorda Project segment comprises the joint venture Sierra Gorda. The Resource BaseRead More Development segment comprises companies involved in the exploration for and evaluation of minerals resources, companies intended to carry out mining. The Support of the Core Business segment comprises companies directly related to the core business. The Other Segments included are the companies of the group not related to the mining industry. Its products include copper in several forms, including cathodes, wire rods, round billets, and granules. The company was founded in 1961 and is headquartered in Lubin, Poland.
