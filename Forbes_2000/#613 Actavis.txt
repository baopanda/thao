Actavis plc operates as an investment holding company. It is a global, integrated specialty pharmaceutical company focused on developing, manufacturing and distributing generic, brand, brand generic, over-the-counter and biosimilar products. The company operates through the following segments: North American Brands, North American Generics and International, and Anda. Actavis was founded on October 1, 2013 and is headquartered in Dublin, Ireland.
