Hokkaido Electric Power Co., Inc. engages in the provision of electricity. It operates through the Electric Power Business and Others segments. The Electric Power Business segment centers on electric utility business. The Others segment deals with electrical and telecommunication works, civil engineering and construction, comprehensive building management, and power plant maintenance, inspection, and repair work. The company was founded on May 1, 1951 and is headquartered in Sapporo, Japan.
