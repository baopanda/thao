The Bank of New York Mellon Corp. operates as a holding company, which engages in the provision of global financial services. It provides financial services for institutions, corporations and high-net-worth individuals, offering investment management and investment services. It operates through the following segments: Investment Management, Investment Services, and Others. The Investment Management segment  offers investment strategies, investment vehicles, and wealth management.
