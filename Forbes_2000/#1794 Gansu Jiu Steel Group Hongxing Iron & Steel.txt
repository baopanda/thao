Gansu Jiu Steel Group Hongxing Iron & Steel Co., Ltd. engages in the production and distribution of iron, steel, and rolled products. Its products include high-speed wires, steel basr, medium platse, hot rolled coils, cold rolled coils, cold rolled plates, and casting billets. The company was founded on April 17, 1999 and is headquartered in Jiayuguan, China.
