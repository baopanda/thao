VTB Bank PJSC is a financial institution that engages in providing a range of banking products and services. It operates through the following segments: Corporate-Investment Banking; Loans and Deposits; Transaction Banking; Mid-Corporate Banking; Retail Banking and Insurance; Treasury, Corporate Center and Other Business; Construction and Development and Other Segments. The company was founded on October 17, 1990 and is headquartered in St. Petersburg, Russia.
