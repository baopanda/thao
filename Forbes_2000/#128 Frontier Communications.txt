Frontier Communications Corp. is a communications company, which provides services to rural areas and small and medium-sized towns and cities in the U.S. It offers a variety of voice, data, Internet, and television services and products. The company provides both regulated and unregulated voice, data and video services to residential, business and wholesale customers. Frontier Communications was founded in 1935 and is headquartered in Stamford, CT.
