Banque Marocaine du Commerce Exterieur SA engages in retail banking, corporate banking and investment activities. It operates domestically and internationally. It operates through the follwing segments: Activity in Morocco, Asset Management, Specialized Financial Services, International Activities and Others. It also offers other activities: Locasom, EAI, CID, Hanouty. The company was founded in 1959 and is headquartered in Casablanca, Morocco.
