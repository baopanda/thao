MMC Norilsk Nickel PJSC engages in the exploration, extraction, refining, and sale of base and precious metals. It produces nickel, palladium, platinum, and copper. It also produces various by-products, such as cobalt, chromium, rhodium, silver, gold, iridium, ruthenium, selenium, tellurium, and sulfur. The company was founded on November 4, 1989 and is headquartered in Moscow, Russia.
