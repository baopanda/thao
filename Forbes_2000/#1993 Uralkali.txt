Uralkali JSC is a vertically integrated potash producer with control over the whole production chain, from potash ore mining, through potassium chloride supply to customers. The company is mainly engaged in the extraction, production and sale of potash fertilizers in the vicinity of the city of Berezniki and Solikamsk, Russia.  Its products include pink muriate of potash, white muriate of potash, and granular muriate of potash. Uralkali was founded in 1930 and is headquartered in Berezniki, Russia.
