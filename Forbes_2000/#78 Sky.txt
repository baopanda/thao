Sky Plc is an entertainment company which provides TV and WiFi services to a range of commercial businesses, including offices, retail outlets, hotels and licensed premises. The company owns and operates Britain and Ireland's portfolio of pay TV channels across entertainment, sports, movies and 24 hour news. It has four main entertainment channels: Sky 1, Sky Living, Sky Atlantic and Sky Arts. Sky was founded on April 25, 1988 and is headquartered in Middlesex, the United Kingdom.
