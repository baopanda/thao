Oil & Gas Development Co. Ltd. engages in the exploration and development of oil and gas resources, including production and sale of oil and gas and related activities. Its operations include exploration, drilling, process & plants, production, reservoir management, engineering support & commercial. Oil & Gas Development Co was founded on March 4, 1961 and is headquartered in Islamabad, Pakistan.
