Hyundai Hysco Co., Ltd. is engaged in the manufacture of steel products. Its products includes cold rolled, steel pipe, and reduce automobile weight products. The cold rolled products offer pickled and oiled coil, hot dip galvanized steel, cold rolled steel, hot dipped and alloyed galvanized steel sheets, EG, and color steel sheets. The reduce automobile weight products provide tailor welded blank, hydroforming, and hot stamping. The company was founded in March, 1975 and is headquartered in Ulsan, South Korea.
