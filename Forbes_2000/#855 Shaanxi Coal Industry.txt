Shaanxi Coal Industry Co., Ltd. engages in the operation, mining, process, management, and sale of coal. Its business activities include coal rail transport; operation, management, investment, and development of new energy. Its products can be categorized into: Power, Chemical and Metallurgical. The company was founded on December 23, 2008 and is headquartered in Xi'An, China.
