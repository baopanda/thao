Motor Oil (Hellas) Corith Refineries SA engages in petroleum refining and energy supply business in Greece. It operates through the following segments: Refinery's Activities, Sales to Gas Stations, and Services. Its products include petroleum and lubricants. The company was founded by Vardis J. Vardinoyannis in 1970 and is headquartered in Maroussi, Greece.
