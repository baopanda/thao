Assicurazioni Generali SpA engages in the provision of insurance and financial products. It operates through the following business segments: Non-Life, Life, and Holding and Other Business. The Non-Life segment provides motor, travel, health, home, and family assistance. The Life segment focuses on savings and protection, health, and supplementary pension insurances.
