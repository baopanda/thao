Deutsche Lufthansa AG provides passenger, freight and cargo airline services. It operates through the following business segments: Passenger Airline Group, Logistics, Maintenance, Repair, Overhaul, Catering, Information Technology Services, and Others. The Passenger Airline Group segment offers passenger services. The Logistics segment provides a range of freight transport services for various cargos, including live animals, valuable cargo, mail, dangerous goods, and temperature-sensitive goods; and road feeder services.Read More The Maintenance, Repair, Overhaul segment focuses on maintenance, repair, overhaul, modification and conversion, and engines and components for passenger aircraft. The Catering segment provides airline catering, and related upstream and downstream in-flight services; in-flight equipment and logistics services; management of onboard service; and in-flight retail. The company was founded on January 6, 1926 and is headquartered in Cologne, Germany.
