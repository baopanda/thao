Svenska Cellulosa AB is engaged in the development and production of hygiene and forest products. It operates through the following business areas: Personal Care, Tissue, and Forest Products. The Personal Care business area includes incontinence care products, baby diapers, and feminine care products. The Tissue business area comprises consumer tissue and Away-From-Home (AFH) tissue for hospitals, large workplaces, restaurants and hotels. The Forest Products business area sells publication papers, packaging papers, pulp,Read More solid-wood products and renewable energy. The company was founded in 1929 and is headquartered in Stockholm, Sweden.
