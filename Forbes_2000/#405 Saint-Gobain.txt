Compagnie de Saint-Gobain SA is engaged in the production and distribution of construction materials. The company operates through the following segments: Innovative Materials, Construction Products, Building Distribution. The Innovative Materials segment offers a unique portfolio of materials and processes for the habitat and industrial markets. The Construction Products segment offers interior and exterior solutions to enhance the comfort of buildings and homes, including plaster, acoustic and thermal insulation, wallRead More facing, roofing and pipe systems. The Building Distribution segment serves the new building, renovation and home improvement markets. Compagnie de Saint-Gobain was founded in 1665 and is headquartered in Courbevoie, France.
