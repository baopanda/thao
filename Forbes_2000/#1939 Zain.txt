Mobile Telecommunications Co. KSC engages in the provision of mobile telecommunications services such as voice, messaging and data in the Middle East and North Africa. It purchases, delivers, installs, manages and maintains mobile telephone and paging systems. It also engages in the investment of surplus funds in securities. The company was founded on June 22, 1983 and is headquartered in Kuwait City, Kuwait.
