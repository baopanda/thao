Israel Corp. Ltd. is a holding company that engages in the initiation, promotion, and development of businesses in local and abroad. The Israel Chemicals Ltd. segment is a multi-national group, operating in the areas of fertilizers and special chemicals. The Oil Refineries Ltd. segment refines crude oil, produces fuel products and raw materials for the petrochemical industry and materials for the plastic industry.
