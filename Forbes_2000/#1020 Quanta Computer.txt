Quanta Computer Inc. manufactures and sells notebook computers and other personal computer-related products. It provides enterprise network systems, home entertainemnt, mobile communication, automotive electronics, and digital home products. Its products include laptop computers, smart phones, servers, digital television sets, car electronics, and wireless devices. The company was founded by Lin Pai Li and Liang Tzu Chen on May 9, 1988 and is headquartered in Taoyuan, Taiwan.
