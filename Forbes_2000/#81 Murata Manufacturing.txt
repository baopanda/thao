Murata Manufacturing Co., Ltd. engages in research, development, manufacture, and sale of electronic components and devices made from fine ceramics. It operates through the following divisions: Components, Modules, and Other. The Components division offers capacitors and piezoelectric products. The Modules segment provides communication modules and power supplies.
