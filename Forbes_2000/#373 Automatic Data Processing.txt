Automatic Data Processing, Inc. provides business outsourcing solutions. It operates through the following segments: Employer Services and Professional Employer Organization Services. The Employer Services segment offers business outsourcing and human capital management solutions, including payroll, benefits administration, talent management, human resources management, time and attendance management, insurance, retirement and compliance & payment.
