Osaka Gas Co., Ltd. engages in the provision of natural gas. It operates through the following segments: Gas, Liquefied Petroleum Gas (LPG), Electricity, and Other Energies, International Energies, and Life and Business Solutions. The Gas segment includes gas and gas equipment sales, gas pipeline construction, and heat supply operations. The LPG, Electricity, and Other Energies segment supplies LPG, electricity, and industrial gases.
