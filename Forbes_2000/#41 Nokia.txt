Nokia Oyj provides network infrastructure, technology and software services. It operates through the following segments: Mobile Broadband, Global Services, and Nokia Technologies. The Mobile Broadband segment engages in the provision of flexible network solutions for mobile voice and data services through its Radio and Core business lines.
