Mitsubishi UFJ Lease and Finance Co., Ltd. engages in the leasing, installment sales, and financial business. It operates through the Customer Finance and Asset Finance segments. The Customer Finance segment includes finance leases, installment sales, loans to individual customers, relating to credit risk management.
