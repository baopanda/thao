Tullow Oil Plc engages in the oil and gas exploration, development and production, and the sale of hydrocarbons and related activities. It holds interests in exploration and production licences across countries in West & North Africa, South & East Africa and Europe, South America and Asia. The company was founded by Aidan Joseph Heavey in 1985 and is headquartered in London, the United Kingdom.
