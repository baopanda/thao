Caltex Australia Ltd. is a transport fuel supplier, convenience retailer and an integrated oil refining and marketing company. The company operates through two segments: Marketing and Supply Chain. The Marketing function promotes and sells caltex fuels, lubricants, specialty products and convenience store goods through a national network of Caltex.
