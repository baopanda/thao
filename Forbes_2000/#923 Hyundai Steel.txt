Hyundai Steel Co. engages in the manufacture and sale of steel products. Its main products include wide flange beams, reinforcing bar, hot rolled steel, steel plate, sections, high speed steel roll, steel casting products for ship parts, track shoe assembly, inverted angle, sheet pile, Asymmetric H-Beams (slim beam), H-Beams for building structure, parallel flange channels and high strength deformed bars. The company was founded on June 10, 1953 and is headquartered in Incheon, South Korea.
