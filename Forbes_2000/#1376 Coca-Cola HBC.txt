Coca-Cola HBC AG operates as a holding company, which engages in the producing, selling and distribution of non-alcoholic beverages. Its products consist of sparkling beverages, still beverages and water, including juices, sports and energy drinks, and other ready to drink beverages such as teas and coffees. It also distributes beer and third party premium spirits in certain of its central and eastern European operations. The company was founded on September 19, 2012 and is headquartered in Zug, Switzerland.
