TOMONY Holdings, Inc. is engaged in the banking and financial services. It operates through the following segments: Banking, Leasing, and Others. The Banking segment provides financial services such as deposits, lending, foreign exchange, investment securities, and bonds. The Leasing segment offers leasing business through TOMONY Lease. The Others segment handles banking operations, software development, and credit cards. The company was founded on April 1, 2010 and is headquartered in Takamatsu, Japan.
