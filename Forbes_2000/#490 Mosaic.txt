The Mosaic Co. engages in the production and marketing of concentrated phosphate and potash crop nutrients. The company operates its businesses through its wholly and majority owned subsidiaries. It operates through the following segments: Phosphates, Potash, and International Distribution. The Phosphates segment owns and operates mines and production facilities in Florida which produce concentrated phosphate crop nutrients and phosphate-based animal feed ingredients, and processing plants in Louisiana which produceRead More concentrated crop nutrients. The Potash segment owns and operates potash mines and production facilities in Canada and the U.S. which produce potash-based crop nutrients, animal feed ingredients and industrial products. The International Distribution segment is consists of sales offices, crop nutrient blending and bagging facilities, port terminals, and warehouses in Brazil, Paraguay, India, and China. The company was founded on October 22, 2004 and is headquartered in Plymouth, MN.
