Galp Energia SGPS SA engages in the exploration and production of oil and gas. It operates through the following segments: Exploration & Production, Refining & Distribution, and Gas & Power. The Exploration & Production segment includes oil resources, reserves, development, and production. The Refining & Distribution segment deals with the supply and trade of oil and biofuels. The Gas & Power segment engages in natural gas distribution and marketing, cogeneration, and electricity trading.
