Nan Ya Plastics Corp. is engaged in the specialty chemicals industry. It operates through the following divisions: Electronic Materials, Petrochemicals, Plastic Products, and Polyester Fiber and Utility Products. The company was founded by YungChing Wang and YungTsai Wang on August 22, 1958 and is headquartered in Taipei, Taiwan.
