Odakyu Electric Railway Co., Ltd. is engaged in the transportation business. It operates through the following segments: Transportation, Merchandising, Real Estate, and Others. The Transportation segment includes railway, bus, taxi, sightseeing boat, and ropeway services. The Merchandising segment manages department stores and supermarkets. The Real Estate segment sells and leases land and buildings. The Others segment ventures in the following businesses: hotel, restaurant, travel, golf, advertising, railway maintenance,Read More motor service station and car dealership, finance and bookkeeping, landscaping and gardening, building management and maintenance, insurance agency, and nursing and child care. The company was founded on May 1, 1923 and is headquartered in Tokyo, Japan.
