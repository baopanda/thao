Hebei Iron & Steel Co., Ltd. engages in the manufacture and process of iron and steel products. Its products include steel, steel billet, and vanadium and titanium products. Its other activities include electric machinery repairing, manufacturing and supplying of oxygen and metallurgy technology services. The company was founded on June 29, 1994 and is headquartered in Shijiazhuang, China.
