Kerry Group Plc engages in the manufacture and distribution of food and beverages. It operates through the Ingredients & Flavours and Consumer Foods segments. Its products include frozen meals, hot and cold pies, processed meats, and dairy spreads. It distributes under the following brands: LowLow, Cheestrings, Dairygold, Charleville, Denny, Richmond, Wall's, and Mattesons. The company was founded in 1972 and is headquartered in Tralee, Ireland.
