Magnit PJSC is a holding company, which engages in the operation of retail stores. It operates through the following formats: Convenience Stores, Hypermarkets, Magnit Family and Cosmetic Stores. It also engages in the food retail market. The company was founded by Sergey Nikolaevich Galitskiy in 1994 and is headquartered in Krasnodar, Russia.
