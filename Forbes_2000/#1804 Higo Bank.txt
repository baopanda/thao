Higo Bank, Ltd. engages in providing commercial banking services. The main business of the Bank is procuring funds from deposits accepted from individual and corporate customers and from financial markets. It operates through the following business divisions: Lending, Securities Investment, and Other. The Lending division involves loans, provided to small- and medium-sized enterprises and individual customers. The Securities Investment division involves investment activities in Japanese government bonds and public debtRead More securities. The Bank also invests and trades in corporate bonds, stocks, foreign securities, and other financial instruments. The Other division involves credit card business, business credit guarantee, and leasing business. The company was founded on July 25, 1925 and is headquartered in Kumamoto, Japan.
