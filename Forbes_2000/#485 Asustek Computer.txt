ASUSTek Computer, Inc. engages in the manufactures and distribution of computers, communication products, and consumer electronics solutions. It offers laptops, personal computers (PCs), broadband products, graphics cards, three-dimension graphics display cards, liquid crystal display monitors, mobile telephones, pocket PCs, server motherboards, server systems, and computer accessories and peripherals. The company was founded on April 2, 1990 and is headquartered in Taipei, Taiwan.
