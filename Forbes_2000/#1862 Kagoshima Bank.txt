Kagoshima
The Kagoshima Bank, Ltd. engages in regional banking business. Its operations are carried out through the following segments: Banking, Leasing, and Others. The Banking segment includes deposits, loans, exchanges, and securities businesses. The Leasing segment provides leasing and lending services. The Others segment includes guarantee and credit card businesses. The company was founded on October 6, 1879 and is headquartered in Kagoshima, Japan.
