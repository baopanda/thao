Mediolanum SpA is a holding company, which primarily engages in the management of its insurance product companies. Through its subsidiaries, it also operates through the following business segments: Italy-Insurance, Italy-Banking, Italy-Asset Management, Spain, and Germany. It also provides other financial services including current accounts, credit cards, mortgages and loans, pensions, managed savings, and general insurance. The company was founded by Ennio Doris in December 1995 and is headquartered in Basiglio, Italy.
