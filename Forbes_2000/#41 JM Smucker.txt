The J. M. Smucker Co. engages in the production and market of fruit spreads, peanut butter and other food products.  The company operates through the following segments: U. S. Retail Coffee, U. S. Retail Consumer Foods and International, Foodservice & Natural Foods.  The U. S. Retail Coffee segment represents the domestic sales of Folgers, Dunkin' Donuts, Millstone, Cafe Bustelo, and Cafe Pilon coffee to retail customers.  The U. S. Retail Consumer Foods segment includes domestic sales of Smucker's, Crisco, Jif, Pillsbury,Read More Eagle Brand, Hungry Jack, and Martha White products. The International, Foodservice & Natural Foods segment is comprised of products distributed domestically and in foreign countries through retail channels, foodservice distributors and operators, for example restaurants, schools and universities, health care operators and health and natural foods stores and distributors. The company was founded by Jerome Monroe Smucker in 1897 and is headquartered in Orrville, OH.
