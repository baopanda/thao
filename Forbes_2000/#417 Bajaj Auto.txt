Bajaj Auto Ltd. manufactures and markets two-wheeler and three-wheeler vehicles. It offers motorcycles, scooters, commercial vehicles, and related spare parts. It operates through the following segments: Automotive, Investments, and Others. The company was founded in November 29, 1945 and is headquartered in Pune, India.
