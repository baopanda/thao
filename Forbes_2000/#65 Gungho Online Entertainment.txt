Gungho Online Entertainment, Inc. is engaged in the development and distribution of online video games. Its operations are carried out through the following segments: Personal Computer Online and Mobile Consumer. The Personal Computer Online segment deals with design, development, and administration of personal computer use online games. The Mobile Consumer segment handles design, creation, and sale of mobile games, mobile contents, and consumer games.
