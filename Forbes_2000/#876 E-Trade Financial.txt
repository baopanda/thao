E*TRADE Financial Corp. engages in the provision of investment and online stock brokerage services. It operates through the following business segments: Trading and Investing; Balance Sheet Management; and Corporate & Other. The Trading and Investing segment offers cash management, bonds, depositary receipts, margin accounts, retirement funds, investment portfolio, and corporate services.
