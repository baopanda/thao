Tsingtao Brewery Co., Ltd. engages in the production and sale of beer and other related business. Through its subsidiaries, it operates through the following segments: Shandong, Huanan, Huabei, Huadong, Dongnan, Hong Kong, Macau and Other Overseas, and Finance Company. The company was founded in August 1903 and is headquartered in Qingdao, China.
