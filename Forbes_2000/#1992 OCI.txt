OCI NV operates as a nitrogen fertilizer producer and engineering and construction contractor. The company operates its business through two segments: Fertilizer and Construction. The Fertilizer segment owns and operates nitrogen fertilizer plants. It also produces anhydrous ammonia, granulated urea, calcium ammonium nitrate and urea ammonium nitrate. The Construction segment engages in the design, construction and maintenance of industrial and commercial infrastructures and buildings, including roads, ports, railroads,Read More hospitals, stadiums, water treatment plants, pumping stations and production sites. The company was founded on January 2, 2013 and is headquartered in Geleen, the Netherlands.
