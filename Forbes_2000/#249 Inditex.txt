Industria de Diseño Textil SA manufactures and sells clothing for men and women. Its activity includes retail distribution of clothing, footwear, accessories and household textile products through various commercial format stores aimed at different targeted sectors of the public. The company is involved in all the stages of the fashion process: from design, manufacturing and logistics, as well as its sales. Its brands include Zara, Pull and Bear, Massimo Dutti, Bershka, Stradivarius, Oysho and Zara Home.
