ENGIE SA engages in the provision of electricity, natural gas, and energy related services. It is active throughout the entire energy value chain, in electricity and natural gas, upstream to downstream in: purchasing, production and marketing of natural gas and electricity; transmission, storage, distribution, management and development of major natural gas infrastructures and energy services. The company was founded on April 8, 1946 and is headquartered in Courbevoie, France.
