Public Power Corp. SA engages in the provision of electricity business. It involves in activities such as production, transmission and distribution of electricity covering the entire Greek country. Its product portfolio is consist of conventional thermal as well as hydroelectric power plants and RES units. The company was founded on August 1950 and is headquartered in Athens, Greece.
