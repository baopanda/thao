Shandong Iron & Steel Co., Ltd. engages in the production and distribution of iron and steel products. Its products are categorized into: Sheets, Profiles, Steel Billets, Ores, Hot and Cold Rolled Steel Products, Power Related Products, Coking Products, and others. The company was founded on December 29, 2000 and is headquartered in Jinan, China.
