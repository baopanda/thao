J.B. Hunt Transport Services, Inc. is a logistics company. The company provides transportation and delivery services to a diverse group of customers across United States, Canada and Mexico. Its offers transportation of full truckload containerized freight, revenue equipment, labor and systems and delivery services. The company operates its business through four segments: Intermodal, Dedicated Contract Services, Integrated Capacity Solutions and Truck. The Integrated Capacity Solutions provides traditional freight brokerage and transportation logistics solutions to customers through relationships with thousands of third-party carriers and integration with its owned equipment.
