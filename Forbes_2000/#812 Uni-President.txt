Uni-President Enterprises Corp. engages in the production and sales of food and feeds as well as investments of other related businesses. It operates through the following segments: Feeds, Foods, Electric Appliance, General Investment, Chain Store, Circulation, and Distribution segment. Its products include milk, noodle, bread, health food, general food, and other related products. The company was founded on August 25, 1967 and is headquartered in Tainan, Taiwan.
