Cimarex Energy Co. is an independent oil and gas exploration and production company. The company operates its business through one segment exploration and production. Its exploration and production activities have been conducted primarily in two main areas: The Mid-Continent region and  The Permian Basin. The Mid-Continent region consists of Oklahoma, the Texas Panhandle and southwest Kansas. The Permian Basin encompasses west Texas and southeast New Mexico.
