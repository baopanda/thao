CC Media Holdings, Inc. operates as a holding company for Clear Channel Communications, which is a global media and entertainment company specializing in radio, digital, outdoor, mobile, live events, and on-demand entertainment and information services for local communities and providing premier opportunities for advertisers. The company operates through three segments: Media & Entertainment, Americas Outdoor Advertising and International Outdoor Advertising. The Media & Entertainment segment provides media andRead More entertainment services via broadcast and digital delivery and also includes the company's national syndication business. The Americas Outdoor Advertising and International Outdoor Advertising segments provide outdoor advertising services in their respective geographic regions using various digital and traditional display types. It consists of billboards, street furniture displays and transit displays. CC Media Holdings was founded in May 2007 and is headquartered in San Antonio, TX.
