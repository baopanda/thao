Rural Electrification Corp. Ltd. provides financial assistance to state electricity boards, state government departments and rural electric cooperatives for rural electrification projects. It provides loan assistance to SEBs, State Power Utilities for investments in rural electrification schemes. Its projects include: Generation-conventional, Generation-Renewable and transmission & Distribution. Rural Electrification was founded on July 25, 1969 and is headquartered in New Delhi, India
