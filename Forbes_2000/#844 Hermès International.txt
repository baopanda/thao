Hermès International SCA engages in the provision of textiles and apparel. Its activities include manufacturing, sale and distribution of apparel products. Its products which include leather goods, ready-to-wear and accessories, silk and textiles as well as perfumes and watches. The company was founded by Thierry Hermès on June 1, 1938 and is headquartered in Paris, France.
