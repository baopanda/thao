Oriental Land Co., Ltd. engages in the operation and management of leisure facilities and hotels. It operates through the following segments: Theme Park, Hotel, and Other. The Theme Park segment operates Tokyo Disneyland and Tokyo Disney Sea. The Hotel segment manages Tokyo Disneyland Hotel, Disney Ambassador Hotel, Tokyo Disney Sea Hotel Mira Costa, and Palm and Fountain Terrace Hotel. The Other segment includes land development operations. The company was founded on July 11, 1960 and is headquartered in Urayasu, Japan.
