Santos Ltd. engages in the exploration, development, production, transportation and marketing of hydrocarbons. It engages in petroleum exploration, the production, treatment and marketing of natural gas, crude oil, condensate, naphtha, liquid petroleum gas, and the transportation by pipeline of crude oil. The company was founded on March 18, 1954 and is headquartered in Adelaide, Australia.
