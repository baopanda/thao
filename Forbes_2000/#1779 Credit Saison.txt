Credit Saison Co., Ltd. engages in the provision of consumer credit services and finance. It operates through the following segments: Credit Service, Lease, Finance, Real Estate-related, and Entertainment. The Credit Service segment is involved in credit card shopping, cash advance business, processing agency business, and servicing business. The Lease segment is involved in providing finance leasing services. The Finance segment is involved in credit guarantees and loans collateralized by real estate properties.
