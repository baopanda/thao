Ecopetrol SA engages in the exploration, development and production of crude oil and natural gas. It operates its business through three segments: Exploration & Production, Refining & Petrochemicals and Transportation & Logistics. The Exploration & Production segment engages in the oil & gas exploration and production activities and its operations include extraction, collection, treatment, storage and pumping or compression of hydrocarbons.
