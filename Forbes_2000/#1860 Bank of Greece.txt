Bank of Greece SA engages in the provision of banking and financial services. Its Euro system-related tasks include formulating monetary policy, managing foreign exchange and reserve assets, issuance of banknotes and coins, overseeing payment and settlement systems, monitoring financial stability, and collecting statistical data. It also supervises credit institutions, insurance and reinsurance firms; manages and operates payment and securities settlement systems; holds and manages the country's official reserve assets suchRead More as foreign exchange and gold reserves; acts as treasurer and fiscal agent of the government; compiles and publishes monetary and credit aggregates; and monitors and analyses the economic conjuncture and monetary policy. The company was founded on October 27, 1927 and is headquartered in Athens, Greece.
