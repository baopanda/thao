The Chugoku Bank, Ltd. engages in the provision of financial services. It operates through the following segments: Banking, Leasing, Securities, and Others. The Banking segment offers financial services such as deposit and lending. The Leasing segment handles leasing services and installment sales of properties and goods. The Securities segment deals with brokerage services for financial instruments.
