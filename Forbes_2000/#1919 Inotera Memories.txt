Inotera Memories, Inc. is engaged in the manufacture and sale of memory semiconductors. Its product categories include information application, communication application, and consumer electronics. It specializes in wafer fabrication, provides manufacturing and services for dynamic random access memory (DRAM) foundry and DRAM memory products. The company was founded on January 1, 2003 and is headquartered in Taoyuan, Taiwan.
