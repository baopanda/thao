Dassault Aviation SA engages in the manufacturing and selling of aircraft. It manufactures and distributes its civilian and military products under the Rafale, Mirage, Falcon, F-Heron, and nEUROn trademarks. The company was founded by Marcel Bloch on December 12, 1936 and is headquartered in Paris, France.
