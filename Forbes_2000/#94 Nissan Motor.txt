Nissan Motor Co., Ltd. engages in the manufacture and sale of automotive products and marine equipment. It operates through the following segments: Automobile and Sales. The Automobile segment manufactures and sells vehicles, forklift, marine equipment, and related parts. The Sales segment handles sales finance and leasing business to support the sales activity of automobile business. The company was founded on December 26, 1933 and is headquartered in Yokohama, Japan.
