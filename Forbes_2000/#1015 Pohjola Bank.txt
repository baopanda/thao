Pohjola Bank Plc is a financial company, which provides banking services. It operates through three segments: Banking, Non-Life Insurance and Asset Management. The Banking segment operates as a corporate bank for large and mid-size companies and provides its domestic customers with a range of financial, investment and payment transfer solutions and also at international level and foreign customers with banking services for business needs in Finland and neighboring regions. The Non-Life Insurance segment provides insuranceRead More solutions for private customers and corporate and institutional customers. The Asset Management segment provides asset management services for institutional and private clients and mainly for the portfolio management of OP mutual funds. The company was founded on May 14, 1902 and is headquartered in Helsinki, Finland.
