Luzerner Kantonalbank AG engages in the provision of banking services. The company operates through the following segments: Private & Commercial Customers, Corporate Clients, Private Banking/Institutional/Trade, and Corporate Center. Its services include portfolio management, mortgages, small and medium scale business financing, investment advice, securities trading, and precious metals. Luzerner Kantonalbank was founded in 1850 and is headquartered in Lucerne, Switzerland.
