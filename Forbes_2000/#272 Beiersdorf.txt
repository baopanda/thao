Beiersdorf AG is engaged in the development, manufacture and distribution of skin and personal care products. The company operates through two segments: Consumer and Tesa. The Consumer segment concentrates on the international skin and body care markets. The Tesa segment manufactures self-adhesive products and solutions for industry, craft businesses, and consumers. Beiesdorf was founded by Paul C. Beiesdorf in 1882 and is headquartered in Hamburg, Germany.
