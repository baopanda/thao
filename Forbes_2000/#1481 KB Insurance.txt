KB Insurance Co., Ltd. engages in the sale of general non-life insurance. It operates through the following business areas: Commercial, Long-Term and Automobile. The Commercial business area includes property, marine, accident and casualty insurances. The Long-Term business offers long-term non-life, property damage, injury, driver, savings, illness, nursing and pension insurances. The Automobile business provides insurances for private use, for hire, for business use of cars bicycles and others.
