Wing Hang Bank Ltd. is an investment holding company, which engages in the commercial banking and financial related services. It operates through three segments: Retail Banking, Corporate Banking and Treasury Activities. The Retail Banking segment include acceptance of deposits, residential mortgages, hire purchase, consumer loans, wealth management, stock brokerage and insurance services. The Corporate Banking segment engages in the advance of commercial and industrial loans, trade financing and institutional banking.
