Nitto Denko Corp. engages in the manufacture and sale of adhesives, optical films, semiconductor devices, electronic, medical, and membrane products. It operates through the following segments: Industrial Tape, Optronics and Others. The Industrial Tape segment manufactures and merchandises engineering plastics, and bonding, joining, surface protection, sealing, and packaging products and equipment. The Optronics segment produces and sells liquid crystal display materials, semiconductor materials, flexible printed circuits,Read More and processing materials. The Others segment includes medical and membrane products. The company was founded on October 25, 1918 and is headquartered in Osaka, Japan.
