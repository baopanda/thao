The Toho Bank, Ltd. engages in the commercial banking business. It operates through the Banking and Others segments. The Banking segment deals with savings, lending, real estate service, and foreign and domestic exchange. The Others segment covers leasing, credit card and credit guarantee operations. The company was founded on November 4, 1941 and is headquartered in Fukushima, Japan.
