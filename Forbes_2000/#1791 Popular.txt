Popular, Inc. engages in the provision of financial banking services. It operates through the Banco Popular de Puerto Rico and Banco Popular North America segments. Its products and services include savings and checking accounts, mortgage and consumer loans, credit cards, cash management, investment banking, and wealth management. The company was founded on October 5, 1893 and is headquartered in San Juan, Puerto Rico.
