The Daishi Bank, Ltd. engages in the provision of financial services. It operates through the following segments: Banking, Leasing, Securities, and Others. The Banking segment includes deposits, loans, security investments, domestic and foreign exchange, and counter sales of government bonds, investment trusts, and insurance. The Leasing segment offers general leasing services. The Securities segment involves trading and operation of securities, and provision of securities investment services.
