OTP Bank Nyrt engages in the provision of commercial banking services. It operates through the following segments: OTP Core Hungary, Russia, Ukraine, Bulgaria, Romania, Serbia, Croatia, Slovakia, Montenegro, Leasing, Asset Management, Others, and Corporate Center. The company was founded on March 1, 1949 and is headquartered in Budapest, Hungary.
