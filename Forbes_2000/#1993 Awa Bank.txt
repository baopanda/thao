The Awa Bank, Ltd. engages in leasing business and provision of financing services. It operates in the following business segments: Commercial Banking and Leasing. The Banking business segment engages in savings deposit, loans, investment securities and domestic/foreign exchange businesses. This segment also sells public bonds, investment trusts and insurance products as well as offers financial products and trust services through its financial brokerage business.
