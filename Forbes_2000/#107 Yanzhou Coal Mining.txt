Yanzhou Coal Mining Co. Ltd. is engaged primarily in the mining business. It is also engaged in the coal railway transpiration business. The company operates its business through the following segments: Coal Mining; Coal Railway Transportation; and Methanol, Electricity and Heat Supply. The Coal Mining segment involves the mining, manufacture, and sale of coal. The Coal Railway Transportation segment provides railway transportation services.
