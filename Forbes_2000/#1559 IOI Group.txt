IOI Corp. Bhd. is an investment holding company, which engages in cultivation and processing of palm oil. It operates through the following segments: Plantation, Property Development, Property Investment, Resource-based manufacturing, and Other operations. The Plantation segment is the cultivation of oil palm and rubber and processing of palm oil. The Property Development segment is the development of properties. The Property Investments division is the investment in shopping malls, office complex and other properties. Other operation includes management and operation of hotels, resorts, landscape services and other operations. The company was founded on October 31, 1969 and is headquartered in Putrajaya, Malaysia.
