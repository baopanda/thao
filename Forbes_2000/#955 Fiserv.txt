Fiserv, Inc. provides financial services technology. It operates through the Payments and Industry Products and Financial Institution Services segments. The Payments and Industry Products segment provides financial institutions and other companies with the products and services required to process electronic payment transactions. The Financial Institution Services segment provides banks, thrifts, and credit unions with account processing services, item processing and source capture services, loan origination and servicingRead More products, cash management and consulting services, and other products and services that support numerous types of financial transactions. The company was founded by Leslie M. Muma and George D. Dalton on July 31, 1984 and is headquartered in Brookfield, WI.
