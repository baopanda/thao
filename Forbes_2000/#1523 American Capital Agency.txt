American Capital Agency Corp. is a real estate investment trust. It primarily invests in agency mortgage-backed securities and collateralized mortgage obligations. The company's investments consist of securities, for which the principal and interest payments are guaranteed by United States Government-sponsored entities, such as the Federal National Mortgage Association and the Federal Home Loan Mortgage Corp., or by U.S. Government agency, such as the Government National Mortgage Association.
