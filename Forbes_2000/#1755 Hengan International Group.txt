Hengan International Group Co., Ltd. operating as an investment holding company, which engages in the trading of personal hygienic products. The company through its subsidiaries engages in the activities of manufacturing, distributing and selling of personal hygiene products, food and snack products and skin care products in China. It operates through five business segments: Tissue Paper Products, Sanitary Napkins Products, Disposable Diapers Products, Food and Snacks Products, and Skin Care Products and Others. HenganRead More International Group was founded in 1985 and is headquartered in Quanzhou, China.
