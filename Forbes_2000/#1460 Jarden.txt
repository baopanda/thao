Jarden Corp. engages in the manufacture, marketing, and distribution of consumer products. It operates through the following segments: Branded Consumables, Consumer Solutions and Outdoor Solutions. The Branded Consumables segment involves in the manufacture or sources, marketing, and distribution of branded consumer products, many of which are affordable, consumable and fundamental household staples. The Consumer Solutions segment manufactures or sources, markets, and distributes household products, including kitchenRead More appliances and home environment products. The Outdoor Solutions segment engages in the manufacture or sources, marketing, and distribution of global consumer active lifestyle products for outdoor and outdoor-related activities. The company was founded by Martin E. Franklin and Ian G. H. Ashken in April 1993 and is headquartered in Boca Raton, FL.
