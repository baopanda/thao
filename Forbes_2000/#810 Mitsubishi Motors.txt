Mitsubishi Motors Corp. engages in the development, design, manufacture, assembly, sales, purchase, and import of vehicles and component parts. It operates through the Automobile and Financial Services segments. The Automobile segment produces and sells passenger cars and parts. The Financial Services segment includes sales finance and leasing. The company was founded on April 22, 1970 and is headquartered in Tokyo, Japan.
