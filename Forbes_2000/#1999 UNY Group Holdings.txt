UNY Co., Ltd. engages in retail business. It operates through the segments: Superstores, Convenience Stores, Specialty Stores, Financial Services, and Other. The Superstores segment deals with general merchandise and food products. The Convenience Stores segment manages franchise systems that offer fast foods and daily-use items. The Specialty Stores segment merchandises apparel, kimono, women's and young casual wear. The Financial Services segment handles credit cards, insurance agencies, and lease transactions. The OtherRead More segment includes facility management, reform and repair business, and real estate leasing. The company was founded by Choju Nishikawa in 1912 and is headquartered in Inazawa, Japan
