Nippon Steel & Sumikin Bussan Corp. engages in the sale, import, and export of steel, industrial supply and infrastructure, textiles, and food products. It operates through the following segments: Steel, Raw Fuel, and Equipment and Industrial Machinery. The Steel segment comprises of steel sheets, steel pipes and tubes, structural steel, rods and bars, stainless steel, titanium, and other steel products. The Raw Fuel segment consists of coal, steel scrap, iron alloy, auxiliary materials for ironmaking and steelmaking, oil,Read More lubricant, coke, and non-ferrous ingots. The Equipment and Industrial Machinery segment includes metal machinery, civil engineering and mining machinery, crane, machine parts, office automation equipment, and other industrial machinery. The company was founded on August 2, 1977 and is headquartered in Tokyo, Japan.
