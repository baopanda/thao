Wüstenrot & Württembergische AG is an independent financial services group, which provides banking and insurance products and services. The company operates its business through the following segments: Loan Savings Bank, Personal Insurance; and Property and Casualty Insurance. It focuses on residential and commercial customers in its core market Germany. The company was founded in 1999 and is headquartered in Stuttgart, Germany.
