Porto Seguro SA engages in the provision of insurance services. It operates through the following segments: Automotive Insurance; Health Insurance Plans; Dental Insurance plans; Health Plans; Complimentary Life Insurance; Insurance for Other Branches; Financial and Asset Consortium; and Others. It provides services to individuals, families, businesses, and government agencies. The company was founded on September 22, 1997 and is headquartered in Sao Paulo, Brazil.
