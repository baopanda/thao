Vedanta Resources Plc is a diversified metals and mining company with interests in aluminum, copper, zinc, lead, silver, iron ore, oil, gas and power. It operates through the following segments: Zinc, Iron Ore, Copper, Aluminium, Power and Oil & Gas. The copper business is operated in India, Zambia and Australia. The zinc business is owned and operated by Hindustan Zinc Ltd. Aluminium business is primarily owned and operated by Bharat Aluminium Co. Iron ore business is operated by Sesa Goa Ltd.
