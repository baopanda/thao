SDIC Power Holdings Co., Ltd. engages in the construction, investment, and management of energy projects. It operates through the following business divisions: Electric Power and Raw Coal. The Electric Power division undertakes the construction and development of power generation projects. The Raw Coal division buys and sells newly mined coal. The company was founded on February 23, 1989 and is headquartered in Beijing, China
