Bharat Petroleum Corp. Ltd. is engaged in the business of refining of crude oil and marketing of petroleum products. Its principal activities are to refine and market petroleum, liquefied petroleum gas and petrochemical products, including middle distillates, light distillates, lubricants, benzene and toluene. The company's segments include Downstream Petroleum i.e. refining and marketing of petroleum products; and Exploration and Production of Hydrocarbons.
