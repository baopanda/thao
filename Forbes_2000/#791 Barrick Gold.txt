Barrick Gold Corp. mines and explores for gold, copper and nickel. The company operates its projects in Canada, the United States, the Dominican Republic, Australia, Papua New Guinea, Peru, Chile, Argentina, Zambia, Saudi Arabia and Tanzania. Its gold operating units are: Cortez, Goldstrike, Pueblo Viejo, Lagunas Norte, Veladero, North America-other and Australia Pacific. The company was founded by Peter D. Munk in 1983 and is headquartered in Toronto, Canada.
