Noble Corp. Plc engages in offshore drilling services for oil and gas industry. It operates through the Contract Drilling Services and Others segments. The Contract Drilling Services segment owns and operates mobile offshore drilling units, and floating production storage and offloading unit. The Others segment includes labor contract drilling services in Canada and Alaska, as well as corporate related items. The company was founded by Lloyd Noble & Art Olson in 1921 and is headquartered in London, United Kingdom.
