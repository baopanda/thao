LG Display Co., Ltd. is engaged in the development, production, and sale of thin film transistor (TFT) liquid crystal display (LCD) panels. It operates through the following business divisions: TFT-LCD, Flexible Display, and Organic Light-Emitting Diode (OLED). The TFT-LCD division provides LCDs for television, mobile, light emitting diode (LED) and three LCDs. The Flexible Display division provides electronic paper display (EPD), plastic EPD, and flexible touch electronic paper. The OLED division offers active-matrix OLEDsRead More (AMOLED) for television. The company was founded in February 28, 1985 and is headquartered in Seoul, South Korea.
