Aspen Pharmacare Holdings Ltd. operates as a supplier of branded and generic pharmaceutical products. Its products includes tablets, capsules, eye drops, injectable products, oral contraceptives, form-filled seals, suppositories, liquids, creams, ointments, infant nutritional products, and pharmaceutical ingredients. The company was founded by Michael Guy Attridge in 1997 and is headquartered in Durban, South Africa.
