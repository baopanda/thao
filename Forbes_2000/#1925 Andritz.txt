Andritz AG engages in the provision of plants, equipment, and services for hydropower stations, pulp and paper business, and steel industry. It operates through the following segments: Hydro, Pulp & Paper, Metals, and Separation. The Hydro segment designs and develops electromechanical equipment, water pumps, and turbo generators for hydropower and thermal power plants. The Pulp & Paper segment offers machines for the process of logs, fibers, and waste paper as well as the production of paper, tissue, and cardboards. TheRead More Metals segment provides presses, automation solutions, and dies for the process and manufacture of steel, copper, and aluminum. The Separation segment engages in animal feed and biomass production. The company was founded by Josef Körösi in 1852 and is headquartered in Graz, Austria.
