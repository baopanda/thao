UCO Bank engages in the business of providing commercial banking services. The bank operates through four segments: Treasury; Corporate and Wholesale Banking; Retail Banking; and Other Banking Operations. It offers personal banking, corporate banking, international banking, rural banking and other services to its customers. The bank was founded by Ghanshyam Das Birla on January 6, 1943 and is headquartered in Kolkata, India.
