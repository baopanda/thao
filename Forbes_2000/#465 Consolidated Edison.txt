Consolidated Edison, Inc. is a holding company, which through its subsidiaries engages in the business of regulated electric, gas, and steam delivery. It operates through the following segments: Consolidated Edison Co. of New York, Inc.; Orange & Rockland Utilities, Inc; Competitive Energy Businesses; and Other. The Consolidated Edison Co. of New York segment involves regulated electric, gas, and steam utility businesses. The Orange & Rockland Utilities segment covers regulated electric and gas utility delivery businesses.Read More The Competitive Energy Businesses segment sells electricity to wholesale and retail customers; provides energy-related products and services; and participates in energy infrastructure projects. The Other segment includes Con Edison Transmission, Inc.; which invests in electric and gas transmission projects. The company was founded in 1823 and is headquartered in New York, NY.
