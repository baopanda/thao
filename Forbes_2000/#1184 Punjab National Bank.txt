Punjab National Bank operates as a commercial bank in India. It operates through the following segments: Treasury, Corporate/Wholesale, Retail Banking, and Other Banking Operations. Its services include personal, social, MSME, agricultural, corporate, international banking and financial services. The company was founded by Punjab Keshari Lala Lajpat Rai on April 12, 1895 and is headquartered in New Delhi, India.
