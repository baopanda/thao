Mercuries & Associates Holding Ltd. engages in the retail business. It operates and manages restaurants, fast food chains, and department stores as well as distributes, trades, and sells tobacco and alcohol. Its other activities include the manufacture, trade, and installation of automatic charging system and computer equipment.  It is also involved in the life insurance business. The company was founded on February 19, 1965 and is headquartered in Taipei, Taiwan.
