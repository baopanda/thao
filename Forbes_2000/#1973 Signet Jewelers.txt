Signet Jewelers Ltd. is a specialty retail jeweler by sales in the US, Canada and UK. The company has one business segment, the retailing of jewelry, watches and associated services. The business is managed as two geographical operation divisions: the U.S. division and the UK division. The U.S. division stores trade nationally in malls and off-mall locations as Kay Jewelers and regionally under a number of well-established mall-based brands. The UK division's stores trade as H.
