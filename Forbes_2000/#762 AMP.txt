AMP Ltd. operates as an independent wealth management company which engages in the provision of insurance, pensions, fund management, banking, and related personal financial services. It operates through the Australian Wealth Management, AMP Capital, Australian Wealth Protection, AMP Bank, New Zealand Financial Services and Australian Mature. The company was founded in 1849 and is headquartered in Sydney, Australia.
