China Vanke Co., Ltd. engages in the provision of real estate. It specialises in property development with commodity housing projects. Its operations are divided into development and management. The Property Development division refers to sale and development of residential properties. The Property Management division offers management and other related services to purchasers and tenants of the company's developed residential properties and shopping arcades as well as those developed by external property developers.
