Burberry Group Plc is a holding company, which designs, produces and sells products under the Burberry brand. It operates through Retail/Wholesale and Licensing business channels. The Retail/Wholesale business channel sells luxury goods through Burberry mainline stores, concessions, outlets and digital commerce, as well as Burberry franchisees and department stores. The Licensing business channel offers global licenses of fragrances, eyewear, timepieces and European children's wear.
