Mylan N.V., is a pharmaceutical company which develops, licenses, manufactures, markets and distributes generic, branded generic and specialty pharmaceutical products. It operates through two segments: Generic and Specialty. The Generic pharmaceutical business is conducted primarily in the U.S. and Canada; Europe; and India, Australia, Japan, New Zealand and Brazil as well as its export activity into emerging markets.
