SCOR SE operates as a reinsurance company. The company operates its business through two segments: SCOR Global P&C and SCOR Global Life. The SCOR Global P&C segment focuses on property and casualty insurance and reinsurance products. The SCOR Global Life segment focuses only on reinsurance products. The company was founded on August 16, 1855 and is headquartered in Paris, France.
