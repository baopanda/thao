Oriental Bank of Commerce Ltd. provides banking and financial services. The company operates its business through four segments: Treasury Operations, Corporate/Wholesale Banking, Retail Banking and Other Banking Business Operations. The Treasury Operations segment consists of dealing in securities and Money Market Operations.
