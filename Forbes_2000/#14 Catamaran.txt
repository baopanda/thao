Catamaran Corp. engages in providing pharmacy benefits management and healthcare information technology solutions. It operates through the Pharmacy Benefits Management (PBM) and Healthcare Information Technology (HCIT) segments. The PBM segment provides electronic point-of-sale pharmacy claims management, retail pharmacy network management, mail and specialty pharmacy claims management, Medicare Part D services, benefit design consultation, preferred drug management programs, drug review and analysis, consulting services,Read More data access and reporting and information analysis. The HCIT segment offers software products for managing prescription drug programs and for drug prescribing and dispensing. The company was founded on March 19, 1993 and is headquartered in Schaumburg, IL.
