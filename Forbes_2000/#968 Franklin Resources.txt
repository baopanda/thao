Franklin Resources, Inc. is a holding company, which engages in the provision of financial and investment management operations. It offers fund administration, sales, distribution, marketing, shareholder servicing, trustee, custody, and fiduciary services. The company was founded by Rupert H. Johnson, Sr. in 1947 and is headquartered in San Mateo, CA.
