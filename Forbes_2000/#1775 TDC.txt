TDC A/S engages in the provision of communications and entertainment solutions. It offers landline telephony, landline broadband, mobility services, pay television and The multi-play segment. Its brands include TDC, Fullrate, YouSee, Telmore, NetDesign, TDC Hosting and Dansk Kabel TV. The company was founded on November 14, 1990 and is headquartered in Copenhagen, Denmark.
