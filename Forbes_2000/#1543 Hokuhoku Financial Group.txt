Hokuhoku Financial Group, Inc. engages in the management and control of its subsidiaries and affiliates. It operates through the following segments: Hokuriku Bank, Hokkaido Bank, and Others. Both Hokuriku Bank and Hokkaido Bank segments offer financial services through banking operations. The Others segment covers the leasing and credit card business. It also manages non-bank subsidiaries. The company was founded on September 26, 2003 and is headquartered in Toyama, Japan.
