TELUS Corp. operates as a telecommunications company. It provides telecommunication services and products, including wireless and wireline voice and data. The company operates through two segments: Wireless and Wireline. The Wireless segment offers data and voice, and equipment solutions. The Wireline segment offers various data services, including Internet protocol, television, hosting, managed information technology, security and cloud-based services, healthcare solutions, and business process outsourcing.
