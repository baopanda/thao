Coca-Cola Enterprises, Inc. markets, produces, and distributes nonalcoholic beverages. It distributes products to customers in Belgium, continental France, Great Britain, Luxembourg, Monaco, the Netherlands, Norway, and Sweden. Its products include Coca-Cola, Diet Coke/Coca-Cola Light, Coca-Cola Zero, Fanta and Capri-Sun. The company was founded on October 2, 2010 and is headquartered in Atlanta, GA.
