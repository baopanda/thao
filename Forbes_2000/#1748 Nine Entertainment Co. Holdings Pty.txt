Nine Entertainment Co. Holdings Ltd. is an Australian media and entertainment company. It operates in the television industry and also in live events and online media industries. The company's business portfolio comprises: Nine Network, Nine Events and, Nine Digital and Ventures. The Nine Network owns free-to-air television licenses in the capital cities of Sydney, Melbourne, Brisbane, Perth and Adelaide as well as in regional Northern New South Wales and Darwin. The Nine Events is a live events business that includesRead More Ticketek. The Nine Digital and Ventures operates Mi9. The company's business roots are back to 1956 and founded on October 16, 2006 and is headquartered in Sydney, Australia.
