MegaFon PJSC engages in the provision of telecommunications services. It offers voice, mobile and fixed-line data transfer; wireless data and wireline broadband services; roaming, short message service, and value-added service; developing business solutions; and sales of mobile phones and other electronics. The company was founded on June 17, 1993 and is headquartered in Moscow, Russia.
