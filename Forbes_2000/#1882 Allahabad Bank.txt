Allahabad Bank operates as a nationalized commercial bank. Its activities include accepting deposits, lending loans and provide financial related services. It provides short and long term loans to agricultural, small scale industries and other priority sectors. The company also offers Internet banking facilities and other banking business to its customers. It operates through three segments: Treasury Operations, Corporate and Wholesale Banking, Retail Banking and Other Banking Business.
