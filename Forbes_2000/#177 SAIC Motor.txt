SAIC Motor Corp. Ltd. engages in the development, manufacture, sale of automobiles and related parts. The company operates through two segments: Vehicles & Parts and Financing. Its business activities include the sale of passenger and commercial vehicles, and components such as engine, transmission, power train, chassis, exterior trim and also offers automotive trade services such as auto financing business. The company was founded on November 24, 1997 and is headquartered in Shanghai, China.
