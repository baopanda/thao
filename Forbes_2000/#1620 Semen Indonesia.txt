PT Semen Indonesia (Persero) Tbk is engaged in the production and distribution of cement. It operates through the Cement Production and Non-Cement Production segments. The Cement Production segment produces different types of cement such as Portland, Special Blended, Super Masonry, Portland Pozzolan, Portland Composite, Oil Well, and Thang Long. The Non-Cement Production segment is involved in limestone and clay mining, cement bag manufacturing, and industrial estate leasing.
