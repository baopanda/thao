Ricoh Co., Ltd. engages in the development, production, sale, and provision of services for imaging, network system solutions, and industrial products. It operates through the following segments: Imaging and Solutions, Industrial Products, and Other. The Imaging and Solutions segment consists of products that are used in the office and production printing environments. The Industrial Products segment manufactures and sells thermal media, optical equipment, semiconductor devices, and electronic components. The Other segmentRead More includes digital cameras, financing, and logistics services. The company was founded by Kiyoshi Ichimura on February 6, 1936 and is headquartered in Tokyo, Japan.
