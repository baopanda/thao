Tsim Sha Tsui Properties Ltd. operates as an investment holding company, which engages in the lease and selling of real estate properties and property development. The company operates through the following business segments: Property Sales, Property Rental, Property Management & Other Services, Hotel Operations, Investments In Securities & Financing. Tsim Sha Tsui Properties was founded on June 20, 1972 and is headquartered in Hong Kong.
