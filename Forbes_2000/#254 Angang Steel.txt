Angang Steel Co., Ltd. engages in the production and sale of steel products. Its products include hot rolled sheets, cold rolled sheets, galvanized steel sheets, colour coating plates, silicon steel, moderately thick plates, wire rods, large steel products and seamless steel pipes. The company products are used in the following industries: automobile, construction, ship-building, home electrical appliances, railway construction and manufacture of pipelines.
