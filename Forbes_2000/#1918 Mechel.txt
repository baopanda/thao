Mechel OAO is a holding company, which engages in the mining and production of coal and steel, iron core, and nickel products. It operates through the following business segments: Mining, Steel, Ferroalloys, and Power. The Mining segment sells metallurgical and steam coal; iron ore; and coke. The Steel segment offers steel products including semi-finished; carbon and special steel long; carbon and stainless flat; wire; forging and stamping products. The Ferroalloy segment provides ferronickel, ferrosilicon, and ferrochrome.Read More The Power Segment generates and supplies electricity. The company was founded on March 19, 2003 and is headquartered in Moscow, Russia.
