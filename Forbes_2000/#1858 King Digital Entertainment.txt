King Digital Entertainment Plc develops and publishes casual games on digital platforms. It provides highly engaging, differentiated entertainment experience where the combination of challenge and progress drives a sense of achievement. King Digital Entertainment products include candy crush saga, pet rescue saga, farm heroes saga, papa pear saga and bubble witch saga.
