Seiko Epson Corp. engages in the development, manufacture, and sale of information equipment. It operates through the following segments: Information Equipment, Device and Precision Products, and Others. The Information Equipment segment offers inkjet printers, page printers, serial impact dot matrix printers, large-format inkjet printers, color image scanners, mini printers, point-of-sale systems printers, projectors, projector panels, label writers, and personal computers. The Device and Precision Products segment offersRead More crystal units, crystal oscillators, quartz sensors, complementary metal-oxide semiconductor large scale integration products, watches, watch movements, plastic corrective lenses, precision industrial robots, integrated circuit handlers, and industrial inkjet equipment. The Others segment engages in the intra-group services and small-and medium-sized liquid crystal display. The company was founded on May 18, 1942 and is headquartered in Suwa, Japan.
