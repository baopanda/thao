Imperial Tobacco Group Plc manufactures markets and sells a range of cigarettes, tobaccos, cigars, rolling papers and tubes. It operates through the following business segments: Tobacco and Logistics. The Tobacco business segment comprises the manufacture, marketing and sale of tobacco and tobacco-related products, including sales to the Logistics business. The Logistics business segment comprises the distribution of tobacco products for tobacco product manufacturers, including Imperial Tobacco, as well as a range ofRead More non-tobacco products and services. The company was founded in 1901 and is headquartered in Bristol, United Kingdom.
