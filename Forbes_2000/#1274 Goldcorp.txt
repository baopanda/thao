Goldcorp, Inc. engages in exploration of gold and other minerals. The company is engaged in the operation, exploration, development and acquisition of precious metal properties in Canada, U.S., Mexico and Central and South America. Goldcorp was founded by Robert Ross McEwen in 1986 and is headquartered in Vancouver, Canada.
