Lawson, Inc. engages in the management and operation of convenience stores. It operates through the Convenience Store and Other segments. The Convenience Store segment includes chains such as Lawson, Natural Lawson, and Lawson Store 100. The Other segment comprises of entertainment, electronic commerce, financial service, and overseas convenience store businesses. The company was founded on April 15, 1975 and is headquartered in Tokyo, Japan.
