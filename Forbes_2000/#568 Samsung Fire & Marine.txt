Samsung Fire & Marine Insurance Co., Ltd. engages in the provision of marine and casualty insurance services. Its products and services include commercial non-life insurance, long-term non-life insurance, auto insurance, loan and finance and other insurance services. In addition, It also provides general insurance, personal pension and retirement pensions. The company was founded on January 26, 1952 and is headquartered in Seoul, South Korea.
