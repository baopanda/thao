SinoPac Financial Holdings Co., Ltd. is a bank holding company that operates through its subsidiaries. Its subsidiaries engage in banking, securities, leasing, bank assurance, venture capital, management consulting, commodity, and call center. The company was founded on May 9, 2002 and is headquartered in Taipei, Taiwan.
