Ziggo NV is a cable operator, which engages in the providing entertainment, information and communication through television, broadband internet and telephony services. It also provides standard TV, digital pay TV, high-speed broadband internet and telephony services to consumers and businesses. The company intends to offer a combination of services in packages, in particular its triple-play offering, the All-in-1 bundle, which offers subscribers the convenience of receiving TV, broadband internet and telephony servicesRead More from a single provider at a lower price than they would pay through three individual service subscriptions. The company was founded on April 1, 2011 and is headquartered in Utrecht, Netherlands.
