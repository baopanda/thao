TPK Holding Co. Ltd. engages in developing, manufacturing, and selling of touch modules, touch display, and conductive glass related products. It offers cover glass, sensor, and touch system. It also provides touch screen solutions for iPhone, iPad, Smartphone, Tablet, ebook, Netbook, point of sale system, kiosk, gaming, and automated teller machine. The company was founded in 2003 and is headquartered in Taipei, Taiwan.
