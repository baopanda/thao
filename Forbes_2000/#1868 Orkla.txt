Orkla ASA engages in the supply of branded consumer goods and concept solutions to the grocery and out-of-home sectors. It operates through the following business areas: Branded Consumer Goods, Orkla Foods, Orkla Confectionery and Snacks, Orkla Home and Personal, Orkla International, and Orkla Food Ingredients. The Orkla Foods area comprises of food businesses. The Orkla Confectionery and Snacks area produces snacks, biscuits, confectionery, and chocolate. The Orkla Home and Personal area supplies personal care products,Read More hygiene and cleaning solutions, dietary supplements and health products, basic textiles, and painting tools. The Orkla International area consists of branded consumer goods. The Orkla Food Ingredients area supplies bakery ingredients. The company was founded on February 27, 1918 and is headquartered in Oslo, Norway.
