Petronas Chemicals Group Bhd. is an investment holding company, which manufactures, markets, and sells petrochemicals. The company operates through three segments: Olefins & Derivatives, Fertilizers & Methanol and Others. The Olefins & Derivatives segment focuses on the supply, trading, manufacturing, marketing, and transportation of olefin and polymer products. The Fertilizers & Methanol segment produces and sells methanol and nitrogen, phosphate and compounded fertilizers. The Others segment supports the petrochemicals'Read More business operations. Petronas Chemicals Group was founded on March 25, 1998 and is headquartered in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia.
