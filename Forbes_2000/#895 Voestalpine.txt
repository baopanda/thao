Voestalpine AG engages in the production, processing, and distribution of steel products. It operates through the five segments: Steel, Special Steel, Metal Engineering, Metal Forming and Other. The Steel segment focuses on the production and processing of flat steel products for the automotive, white goods, and construction industries. The Special Steel segment manufactures long steel products, medium-wide strip steel, open-die forgings, and drop forgings. The Metal Engineering segment manufactures rails and turnoutRead More products, rod wire, drawn wire, prestressing steel, seamless tubes, welding filler materials, and semi-finished products. The Metal Forming segment provides special tubes and sections, special strip steel, and complex components for the automotive industry. The Other segment provides coordination services and assistance to the subsidiaries. The company was founded on May 13, 1938 and is headquartered in Linz, Austria.
