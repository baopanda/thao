EVRAZ Plc is a global, vertically-integrated, steel, mining and vanadium business with operations in the Russian Federation, Ukraine, the Czech Republic, Italy, the USA, Canada and South Africa. The company operates its business through four segments: Steel Production, Mining, Vanadium Products and Other. The Steel Production segment includes the production of steel and related products at eleven steel mills. The Mining segment includes iron ore and coal mining and enrichment. The Vanadium Products segment includes theRead More extraction of vanadium ore and production of vanadium products and also includes Vanadium slag arising in the steelmaking process. The Other operations segment includes energy-generating companies, seaports, shipping and railway transportation companies. EVRAZ was founded on September 23, 2011 and is headquartered in London, the United Kingdom.
