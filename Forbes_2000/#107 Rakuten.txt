Rakuten, Inc. engages in the internet services business. It operates through the following segments: Internet Services, Internet Finance, and Others. The Internet Services segment deals with advertising and content sales in internet shopping mall, various e-commerce sites, hotel reservation sites, and portal sites. The Internet Finance segment handles banking and securities, credit card related services, and electronic money (e-money) businesses.
