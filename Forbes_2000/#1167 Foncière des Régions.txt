Foncière des Régions SA owns, operates and manages real estate properties. It designs real estate solutions. It also owns and manages hotel. The firm operates through following segments: France and Italy Offices, Service Sector, Residential in France and Germany, and Car Parks. The Service sector segment includes operations in the hotel, retail and health sectors held by Foncière des Murs. The company was founded on December 2, 1963 and is headquartered in Metz, France.
