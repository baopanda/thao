Kingfisher Plc operates as home improvement retailer. It engages in the supply of home improvement products and services. Its operates through the United Kingdom & Ireland and Other International geographical segments. The Other International segment consists of Poland, China, Germany, Portugal, Romania, Russia, and Spain. Its main retail brands are B&Q, Castorama, Brico Dépôt, and Screwfix. The company was founded in 1982 and is headquartered in London, the United Kingdom.
