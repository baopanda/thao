Almarai Co. Ltd. engages in the manufacture and trade of dairy, juice, bakery and poultry products. Its brands includes Almarai, Alyoum, L'usine, 7Days, and Nuralac. The company was founded by Sultan bin Mohammed bin Saud Al Kabeer in 1977 and is headquartered in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia.
