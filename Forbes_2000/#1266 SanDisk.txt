SanDisk Corp. engages in the design, development, manufacture, and marketing of digital storage products. Its products include enterprise solutions, client solid state drive solutions, embedded products, removable products, and wafers and components. The company was founded by Eli Harari and Sanjay Mehrotra on June 1, 1988 and is headquartered in Milpitas, CA.
