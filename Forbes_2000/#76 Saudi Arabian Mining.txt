Saudi Arabian Mining Co. engage in the exploration and development of mineral and metal resources. It also trades and processes minerals and metals. Its products include gold and base metals, phosphate, aluminum, and industrial minerals. It also builds public infrastructure such as railways and ports. The company was founded on March 23, 1997 and is headquartered in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia.
