TAURON Polska Energia SA is a holding company, which is engaged in the distribution of electricity. It operates through the Sales and Holding Activity segments. It is also involved in the following activities: operation of central companies and holdings, excluding financial holdings; electric energy trading; wholesale of fuel and derivative products such as coal and biomass trading; and gas fuel trading. The company was founded on December 6, 2006 and is headquartered in Katowice, Poland.
