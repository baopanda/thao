Porsche Automobil Holding SE engages in the development, manufacture, and trade of vehicles. It operates and distributes its products under the following brands: Volkswagen Passenger Cars, Audi, SEAT, ŠKODA, Bentley, Bugatti, Lamborghini, Porsche, Ducati, Volkswagen Commercial Vehicles, Scania, and MAN. The company was founded on June 26, 2007 and is headquartered in Stuttgart, Germany.
