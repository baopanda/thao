Saudi Basic Industries Corp. engages in the manufacture of petrochemicals. It produces chemicals, plastics, agri-nutrients, metals, resins, specialty film and sheet, polymershapes, glazing solutions, and specialty additives and intermediates. The company was founded on September 6, 1976 and is headquartered in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia.
