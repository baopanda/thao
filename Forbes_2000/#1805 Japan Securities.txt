Japan Securities Co., Ltd. engages in the provision of lending and financing services. It operates through the following segments: Securities Finance, Trust Banking, and Real Estate Management. The Securities Finance segment provides loans for margin transactions, bond financing and general loans, and securities lending services. The Trust Banking segment deals with banking and securities trust businesses. The Real Estate Management segment manages leasing of real estate properties.
