Prada SpA operates as a holding company, which engages in the manufacture and distribution of luxury goods. Its products include leather goods, handbags, footwear, apparel, accessories, eyewear, and fragrances. It markets its products under the following brands: Prada, Miu Miu, Church's, and Car Shoe. The company was founded by Mario Prada in 1913 and is headquartered in Milan, Italy.
