Assa Abloy AB engages in the supply of intelligent lock and security solutions. It operates through the following business division: EMEA, Americas, Asia Pacific, Global Technologies and Entrance Systems. The first three divisions are based on products sold in local markets, while the last two are products sold worldwide. The company was founded in 1994 and is headquartered in Stockholm, Sweden.
