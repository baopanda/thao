BG Group Plc engages in the exploration, development and production of natural gas and oil. The company is also involved in the power generation business. It operates through Upstream and LNG Shipping & Marketing segments. The Upstream segment comprises exploration, development, production, liquefaction and marketing of hydrocarbons with a focus on natural gas.
