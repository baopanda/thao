Diageo Plc produces and distributes beverage alcohol with spirits, beer and wine brands. Its spirits, beer and wine brands include Johnnie Walker, Crown Royal, JeB, Buchanan's, Windsor and Bushmills whiskies, Smirnoff, Ciroc and Ketel One vodkas, Captain Morgan, Baileys, Don Julio, Tanqueray and Guinness.
