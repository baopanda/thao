Compal Electronics, Inc. engages in the research, development, design, production, and sale of computing, communications, and consumer products. Its products include notebook PCs, Liquid-Crystal display products, smart device, Ultrabook PCs, 2-in-1 PCs, All-In-One (AIO) PCs, Tablet PCs, Servers, Auto Electronics (AE), Smart Home products, Public Displays, Smart Phones, Smart Accessory, and Wearable Devices. The company was founded by Chau Ing Hsu on June 1, 1984 and is headquartered in Taipei, Taiwan.
