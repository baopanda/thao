Hanwa Co., Ltd. engages in the sale of iron and steel products. It operates through the following segment: Steel, Metal and Alloy, Non-Ferrous Metal, Food Products, Petroleum and Chemical, and Others. The Steel segment engages in the sale of steel bars, building materials, sheets, pipes, wires, and other steel products. The Metal and Alloy segment includes nickel, chromium, manganese, silicon, and ferroalloys. The Non-Ferrous Metal segment offers copper, aluminum, zinc, lead, nickel, and chromium. The Food Products segmentRead More includes shrimp, crab, salmon, herring roe, mackerel, horse mackerel, capelin, and various processed foods. The Petroleum and Chemical segment provides petroleum products, industrial chemicals, and other chemicals. The Others segment handles lumber and plywood, machineries, and overseas sale businesses. The company was founded on April 1, 1947 and is headquartered in Tokyo, Japan.
