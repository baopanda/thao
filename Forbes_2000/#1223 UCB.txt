UCB SA engages in the research, development, and commercialization of pharmaceutical and biotechnology products. It offers medicinal products for central nervous system and immunology disorders under the Cimzia, Xyzal, Zyrtec, Tussionex, Lortab, Vimpat, Keppra, and Neupro. The company was founded by Emmanuel Janssen on January 18, 1928 and is headquartered in Brussels, Belgium.
