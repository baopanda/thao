Vonovia SE is a holding company, which engages in the residential real estate business. It operates through the Rental and Sales segments. The Rental segment comprises activities relating to residential property management, lease management, maintenance and modernization, and tenant assistance. The Sales segment refers to all activities relating to the sale of residential and other units including single unit sales, which comprises of sales of condominium units; and single family homes and non-core divestitures, whichRead More include multi-family residential buildings and commercial properties.
