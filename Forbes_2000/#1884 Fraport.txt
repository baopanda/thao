Fraport AG Frankfurt Airport Services Worldwide is engaged in the ownership and management of a commercial international airport. It offers airfreight and passenger transportation, aircraft handling and apron management services. It also invests in real estate development, airport retailing, and intermodal linkage of transportation. The company was founded on July 2, 1924 and is headquartered in Frankfurt, Germany.
