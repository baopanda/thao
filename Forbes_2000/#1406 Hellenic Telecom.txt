Hellenic Telecommunications Organization SA provides telecommunication services to businesses, consumers and government agencies. It offers products and services, from broadband services, fixed and mobile telephony to satellite, high-speed data communication and leased line services. The company operates in Greece, Romania, Serbia, Bulgaria and Albania. It also offers mobile telephone, international communication, wireless and satellite communication and consultancy services through its subsidiaries.
