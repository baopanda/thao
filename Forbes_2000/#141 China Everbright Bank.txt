China Everbright Bank Co. Ltd. engages in the provision of banking and financial services. It operates through the following segments: Corporate Banking, Retail Banking, Treasury Business, and Others. The Corporate Banking segment offers corporate loans and advances, trade financing and deposit taking activities; agency services, corporate wealth management services, consulting and advisory services, remittance and settlement services, custody services, and guarantee services. The Retail Banking segment provides personalRead More loans and deposit taking activities, bank card business, personal wealth management services, remittance services, and securities agency services. The Treasury Business segment includes inter-bank money market transactions, repurchases transactions, and investments. The Others segment represents equity investments and related income. The company was founded on August 18, 1992 and is headquartered in Beijing, China.
