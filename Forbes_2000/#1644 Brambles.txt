Brambles Ltd. provides supply chain support services and information management solutions. It provides pallet and container pooling solutions through the CHEP and IFCO brands. It provides pooling Solutions and associated services, focusing on the outsourced management of returnable pallets, crates and containers. The company operates through the following segments: Pallets, Reusable Plastic Crates and Containers. The Pallets segment comprises of all CHEP pallet operations in three regions, including the IFCO palletRead More management services business in the U.S. and the lean logistics business in Europe and Asia-Pacific. The Reusable Plastic Crates segment provides reusable plastic crates operations under both the IFCO and CHEP brands. The Containers segment comprises of global intermediate bulk container and automotive container operations, the CHEP catalyst and chemical containers business and the newly-formed CHEP aerospace solutions. The company was founded by Walter Edwin Bramble in 1875 and is headquartered in Sydney, Australia.
