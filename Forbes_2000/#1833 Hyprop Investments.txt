Hyprop Investments Ltd. is a real estate investment trust, which owns and operates shopping centers in South Africa. The company operates through the following business segments: Super Regional Malls, Large Regional Malls, Value/Lifestyle Centers, Offices and Listed Property Securities. Hyprop Investments was founded in 1987 and is headquartered in Rosebank, South Africa.
