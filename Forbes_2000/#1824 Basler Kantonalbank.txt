Basler Kantonalbank provides banking and other financial services. It operates services through its divisions, which include Private Accounts, Investments, Corporate Accounts, Trading, and Corporate Center. The Private Accounts segment involves itself with non-commercial and private deposits. The Investments division engages in investing the bank's and its customers' money in securities. The Corporate Accounts division deals with corporate deposits. The Trading division handles the purchase and/or sale of equities. TheRead More Corporate Center division is responsible for the day-to-day operations of the corporate office of the bank. The company was founded on October 4, 1899 and is headquartered in Basel, Switzerland.
