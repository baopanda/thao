Meiji Holdings Co., Ltd. engages in the management of its subsidiaries with business activities focusing on food and pharmaceutical production. It operates through the Food and Pharmaceutical segments. The Food segment manufactures and sells confectionery, ice cream, milk, dairy, cheese, beverage, and health food products. The Pharmaceutical segment manufactures and sells ethical pharmaceuticals, agricultural chemicals, and veterinary drugs. The company was founded on April 1, 2009 and is headquartered in Tokyo, Japan.
