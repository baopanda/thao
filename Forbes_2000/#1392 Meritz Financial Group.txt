Meritz Financial Group, Inc. is a holding company that engages in the provision financial services. It operates through the following businesses: Meritz Fire and Marine Insurance, Meritz Securities, Meritz Asset Management, Meritz Financial Services, Meritz Business Service, and Meritz Capital. The company was founded on March 28, 2011 and is headquartered in Seoul, South Korea.
