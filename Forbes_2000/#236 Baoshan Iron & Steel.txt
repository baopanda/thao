Baoshan Iron & Steel Co., Ltd. engages in the manufacture and sale of iron and steel products. Its products include carbon steel, stainless steel, and special steel. The carbon steel consists of bar, heavy plate, hot rolled steel sheet, cold rolled steel sheet, wire rods, and pipe tube. The stainless steel covers hot rolled steel sheet and cold rolled steel sheet. The special steel supplied in forms of cake, ring, rod, tube, wire, strip, forging, and casting.
