United Utilities Group Plc provides water and sewage services. It collects water from catchment land and involves in property management services. The company also offers various value added services comprising waste to energy consultancy; engineering advisory services on design, build, and operation of on-site treatment plants; and rainwater harvesting and greywater recycling services.
