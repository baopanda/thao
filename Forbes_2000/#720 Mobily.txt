Etihad Etisalat Co. engages in the provision of mobile phone and internet services. It establish and operate mobile wireless telecommunication and fiber optics networks. The company also install and operate telephone networks, terminals, and communication unit systems in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. It operates through the following business divisions: Personal, Business, and Carriers. The company was founded on August 10, 2004 and is headquartered in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia.
