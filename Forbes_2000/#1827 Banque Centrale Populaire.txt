Banque Centrale Populaire SA operates as a commercial bank. Its activities include the management of cash surpluses from the Banques Populaires Régionales, banking intermediation, and management services of interests to the organizations of Crédit Populaire Maroc. The company was founded on February 2, 1961 and is headquartered in Casablanca, Morocco.
