Makita Corp. engages in the production and sales of electric power tools, stationary woodworking machines, air tools, garden tools, and household tools. Its products includes Cordless, Drilling, Impact Drilling, Cutting(New Material), Cutting(Metal), Grinding, Sawing, Planing, Pneumatic Tolls, Outdoor Power Equipment, Dust Extraction, and Accessories. The company was founded on March 21, 1915 and is headquartered in Anjo, Japan.
