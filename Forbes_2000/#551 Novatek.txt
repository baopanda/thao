Novatek OAO engages in the exploration, development, production, processing, transportation, acquisition, and marketing of natural gas, gas condensate, crude oil, and related oil products. Together with its subsidiaries, it operates through the Exploration, Production, and Marketing segments The company was founded by Leonid Viktorovich Mikhelson on August 16, 1994 and is headquartered in Tarko-Sale, Russia.
