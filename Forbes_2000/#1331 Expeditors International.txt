Expeditors International of Washington, Inc. engages in the provision of global logistics services. It offers airfreight services, ocean freight and ocean services, and customs brokerage and other services. The firm also provides customer solutions such as order management, time-definite transportation, warehousing and distribution, temperature-controlled transit, cargo insurance, and customized logistics solutions.
