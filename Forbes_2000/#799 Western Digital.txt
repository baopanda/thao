Western Digital Corp. develops, manufactures and provides data storage devices and solutions, which address the evolving needs of the information technology industry and the infrastructure that enables the proliferation of data in virtually every industry. The company product portfolio includes hard disk drives, solid-state drives marketed under the HGST, WD and G-Technology brands.
