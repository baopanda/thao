Anglo American Plc is a global mining company, which engages in the exploration and mining of precious base metals and ferrous metals. Its portfolio of mining businesses includes span bulk commodities, including iron ore and manganese, metallurgical coal and thermal coal, base metals and minerals, copper, nickel, niobium and phosphates, and precious metals and minerals. The company was founded on May 24, 1999 and is headquartered in London, the United Kingdom.
