PT Bank Danamon Indonesia Tbk provides commercial banking services. It operates through the following segments: Retail, Mid Size, and Wholesale. The Retail segment includes micro business, credit card, shariah, insurance business, consumer financing, pawn broking, and retail banking. The Mid Size segment focuses on small medium enterprise and commercial businesses. The Wholesale segment deals with corporate banking, financial institution, and treasury services.
