Korean Air Lines Co., Ltd. engages in operating scheduled air transportation services. It provides air transportation services such as passenger, cargo, building lease, maintenance and training services. In addition, It also supplies aerospace, catering, in-flight sales and Limousines. The company was founded on June 19, 1962 and is headquartered in Seoul, South Korea.
