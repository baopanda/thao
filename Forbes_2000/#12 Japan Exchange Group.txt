Japan Exchange Group, Inc. a holding company which engages in the operation of financial instruments exchange. It provides market infrastructure for financial instruments including financial instrument exchanges. It also offers investors a venue to manage their financial assets and listed companies a platform to raise the funds. The company was founded on April 1, 1949 and is headquartered in Tokyo, Japan.
