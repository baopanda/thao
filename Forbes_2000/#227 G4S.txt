G4S Plc is a integrated security company, which specializes in providing security products, services and solution. The company provides its products and services in three divisions: Secure Solutions and Cash Solutions and Care & Justice. The Secure Solutions provides services to both commercial and governments customers. The Cash Solutions offers outsourcing of cash cycle management for central banks, financial institutions and retailers. The Care & Justice segment offers highly specialised services to central and localRead More governments and government agencies and authorities. G4S was founded in 2004 and is headquartered in Crawley, the United Kingdom.
