HUGO BOSS AG engages in the design, manufacture, and sale of clothing and accessories. Its products include children's wear, sportswear, shoes, leather accessories, fragrances, watches, and eyewear. It markets and distributes under the following brands: BOSS, HUGO, and HUGO BOSS. The company was founded by Hugo Ferdinand Boss in 1924 and is headquartered in Metzingen, Germany.
