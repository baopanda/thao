Encana Corp. is a natural gas company. It focuses on developing its portfolio of diverse resource plays producing natural gas, oil and natural gas liquids. Its operations also include the marketing of natural gas, oil and natural gas liquids. It operates through three reportable segments: Canadian, the United States of America and Market Optimization. The Canadian operations segment engages in the exploration, development, and production of natural gas, oil and natural gas liquids within Canada. The Market Optimization segment includes third party purchases and sales of product to provide operational flexibility for transportation commitments, product type, delivery points and customer diversification. The company was founded in 2002 and is headquartered in Calgary, Canada.
