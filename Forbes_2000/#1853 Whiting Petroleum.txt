Whiting Petroleum Corp. operates as an oil and gas company, which is engaged in acquisition, exploration and development of crude oil, natural gas and natural gas liquids. It has operations in the Permian Basin, Rocky Mountains, Mid-Continent, Gulf Coast and Michigan regions of the United States. Whiting Petroleum was founded by Kenneth R. Whiting and J. Bert Ladd in January 1980 and is headquartered in Denver, CO.
