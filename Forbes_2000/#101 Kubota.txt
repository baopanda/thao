Kubota Corp. engages in the manufacture and sale of agricultural and construction machinery and equipment. It operates through the following segments: Machinery; Water and Environment; and Others. The Machinery segment consists of agricultural or farm equipment and products, engines, and construction machinery. The Water and Environment segment includes pipe-related products such as ductile iron pipes, plastic pipes, valves, and pumps; environment-related products; and social infrastructure-related products such asRead More industrial castings, spiral welded steel pipes, vending machines, electronic weighing systems, precision and air-conditioning equipment. The Others segment offers design, construction, and residential equipment manufacturing services. The company was founded by Gonshiro Kubota in February 1890 and is headquartered in Osaka, Japan.
