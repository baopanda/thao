Hunan Valin Steel Co. Ltd. manufactures steel tube and wire. Its principal activities are manufacturing, processing and selling smelted steel, rolled steel, thread steel, small and medium-scale steel materials, ferrous metals, non-ferrous metals and related products. Major products include wire materials, seamless steel pipes, railway materials, steel base, copper plate pipes and aluminum pipe wire. The company was founded on April 29, 1999 and is headquartered in Changsha, China.
