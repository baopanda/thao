Transneft engages in the operation of the oil pipeline system. Its main activates include transfer, coordination and management of transportation of oil and oil products through pipelines within Russia and abroad, and preventive, diagnostic, and emergency rehabilitation work on oil and petroleum products. The company was founded on August 14, 1993 and is headquartered in Moscow, Russia.
